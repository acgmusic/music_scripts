Notes

Double compilation by Michel Barouille including 20 of his best TV credits and songs (on the Disc 1) but also 20 songs from his personal repertoire (on the Disc 2).
It includes a new version of the 1st French Ending Theme from TV series "Choudenshi Bioman" (Disc 1-20) recorded especially for this album.

All vocals by Michel Barouille,
except Disc 2-19 in duet with Valérie Barouille
