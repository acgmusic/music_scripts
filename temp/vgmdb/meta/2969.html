Notes

- "Hey Hey Why?" is a song featured only on the Sega Ages version of Fantasy Zone, released only on the Sega Saturn in Japan.
- "Bonus Track 1" contains the Sega Saturn theme song, "Sega Saturn, Shiro!" It is a pun of the mascot, Segata Sanshiro's, name, and basically means "You must play Sega Saturn!"
- "Bonus Track 2" = contains the Japanese Dreamcast theme song, "Ukase wa Dreamcast."
