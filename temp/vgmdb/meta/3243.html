Notes

Original soundtrack for the Konami-made "Esper Dream" games, two action-RPGs for Famicom.

Tracks 01-37 - Esper Dream 2: Aratanaru Tatakai
Sound: Otsumami Matsuo, Manami Matsumae (Uncredited)

Tracks 38-49 - Esper Dream
Music: Charming Yamako

Tracks 50-52 - Esper Dream 2 CM (Commercial themes)
Vocals: Nazo² Suzuki (50)
Composition: Nazo² Suzuki (52)
Arrangement: Nazo² Suzuki, TAPPY (52)


Producer: Shigeru Fukutake (Konami Kukeiha Club)
Art directed & Sound Produce: Konami Kukeiha Club
Director: Nazo² Suzuki (Konami Co., Ltd.), Jun Otsuki (King Record)
Recording Studio: STUDIO YOU (Osaka), STUDIO SOUND DALI (Tokyo)
Engineer: Shigeru Yamamoto (STUDIO YOU), Yasuhito Ikeda (STUDIO SOUND DALI)
Masttering Engineer: Seiji Kaneko (King Record)
Designer: Yoshinobu Yamaguchi (works D), 池田康史(works D)
