Notes

Composer:
  Masakazu Sugimori (Disc 1)
  Akemi Kimura (Disc 2)

The original print was recalled on 2004/03/30 due to an error in track 1-01. The first three seconds are cut short (the length for the original print is 0:36, while the reissue is 0:39).
The publisher offered a replacement to the purchasers, delivered on 2004/04/10. The catalogue number for the new print was not changed.
