Notes

V-Ron Media, Caitlin Ayala, and Joshua Iyer set out on a quest to explore the mines and complete the GameGrooves co-op album challenge. Grab your pickaxe or slingshot of choice and embark on a journey deep in the mines. Bring these six covers from the indie hit "Stardew Valley" in your inventory and you're bound to have a successful trip to The Mines!
