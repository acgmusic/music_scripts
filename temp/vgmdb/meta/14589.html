Notes

Comic Market 76 release.

Music by dai*

Tracks 1.12, 1.17, 3.21 and 3.22 are not shown on the printed tracklist.
1.12, 1.17 are short transition tracks.
3.21 is an instrumental original track.
3.22 is a vocal version of happiness of marionette by nayuta.**
*dai is only creditted in the individual track credits, but is creditted as producer for the release, and creditted as composer in the game's sound test for all other non-vocal tracks of this release.
**nayuta is creditted for the vocal version of "happiness of marionette" in the general staff credits. This is for the untitled D3-M22 track, and not D1-M29, which is instrumental.
