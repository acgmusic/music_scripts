Notes

Tenchisouzou is an album produced for an art gallery by Seiji Fujishiro titled "Märchen Collection" which featured paintings of the world's creation.

Composer, Arranger, Conductor: Yoko Kanno

1st Violin: Toshihiro Nakanishi (solo), Hijiri Kuwano (solo), Jun Takeuchi, Chieko Kinbara, Tatsuo Ogura, Masahito Ueda, Etsuko Hara, Kuniyo Fukami

Note: The melody from "The Tower of Babel" was later re-used by Kanno in "Sóra" from her soundtrack for Escaflowne (2000).
