Notes

Included with the Australian Fall of Man Premium Edition of the game.

Notes from Parent Album:
Enclosed with "Shin Megami Tensei V" first press limited edition (ATS-02111 / JAN: 4984995904559).

Disc 1: Shin Megami Tensei Series String Quartet Arrangement
Disc 2: Shin Megami Tensei V Soundtrack

2 CDs, 35 tracks – "A selection of songs from Shin Megami Tense I to IV, arranged by a string quartet with a prestigious and spiritual atmosphere. And with the Shin Megami Tensei V Early Access Mini Soundtrack, this set is a music album packed with the total wisdom of the series."
