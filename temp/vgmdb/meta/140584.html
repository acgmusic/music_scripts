Notes

Steel thyself! A massive community undertaking years in the making, CHAMPIONS pays tribute to Jake "virt" Kaufman's masterpiece, Shovel Knight, and all of its accompanying expansions. From styles ranging from classical and rock to jazz and choir, there's a bit of everything and a lot of fun for fans of Shovel Knight and its music!

Available everywhere: scarletmoon.to/champions/
