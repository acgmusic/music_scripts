Notes

Virtual Video Game Orchestra is proud to present our inaugural project, "Snake Eater" from Metal Gear Solid 3, featuring 98 submissions from the video game music community.

Music by Harry Gregson-Williams and Norihiko Hibino (日比野 則彦)
Arranged and Edited by Virtual Video Game Orchestra
Starring Red Hayate as JOIE ZHOU IMPERSONATOR

The purpose of VVGO is to provide a fun and accessible virtual community of musicians from around the world by performing video game music.
All instruments, musical backgrounds, and skill levels are welcome to join!
