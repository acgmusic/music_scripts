"I want to extend a heartfelt thank you to both my music team and the people who supported us at Warner Bros. The incredible attitude and work ethic everyone brought to the table during the creation of this score made it feel effortless during a decidedly trying time in the world. And to my friend Matt, what can I say? You are the best of the best. The care and dedication you put into your work is inspiring beyond words and always pushes me to go beyond where I've been. Between monsters, vampires, apes and now the world's greatest detective, I couldn't ask for a better partner in crime for the work we have done together. Rest up, pal, I'm looking forward to what's next!
- Michael Giacchino, Jan 2022.
