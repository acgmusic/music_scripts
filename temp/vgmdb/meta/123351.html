Notes

In celebration of its 20th anniversary, here's the complete soundtrack CD for the 1987 live action movie "The Red Spectacles" directed by Mamoru Oshii.

This CD includes bonus tracks not previously recorded on the original releases (LP: K20G-7317 and CD: K30X-7103).
The main title has also been re-recorded on track 25.


01~02: "Opening Theme"
03~05: "Kikou Keiji no Eikou to Botsuraku"
06~08: "Buffoon"
09~11: "Desperate Situation"
12~15: "1998-nen 6-gatsu 15-nichi"
16~18: "Mazu no Shuuen"
19~25: BONUS TRACKS
