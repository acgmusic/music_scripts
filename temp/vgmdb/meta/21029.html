Notes

This is actually a reprint of 28XA-201 as part of the Game Sound Legend Series. The tracklisting is different because the medley tracks were broken down into their individual pieces. This printing also includes a carrying case, designed to hold volumes 1 through 4 of the Konami Game Music Collection series.

Another reprinting is supposed to have occured with catalogue number SCDC-00069, but there's no conclusive evidence...
