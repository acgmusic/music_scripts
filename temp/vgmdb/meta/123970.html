Notes

- Quadruple 180g vinyl on frosted clear with green, orange, red and blue splatter
- 12pg booklet featuring island maps, screenshots and extensive liner notes by composer Tomoya Ohtani
- Four illustrated inner sleeves
- Four lithographic art prints, including both the Japanese and Western game covers
- Packaged in a custom-made hardboard slipcase
- Includes 26 tracks (every movement/variation of each island's BGM)
