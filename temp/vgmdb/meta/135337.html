Notes

Trance official album commemorating the 25th anniversary of "Fist of the North Star" (aka "Hokuto no Ken") by DJs such as DJ KOO (TRF) and artists including original singer CRYSTAL KING.
An extra track includes photo data for PC and famous lines and voice data from "Fist of the North Star" by voice actors from the event "Live 5pb." held on November 22, 2008.
