Notes

This box set contains the original soundtrack and symphonic arrangements for the Nausicaä, Arion and Laputa series.  It also contains a full color 34 page booklet with interviews of Joe Hisaishi, Hayao Miyazaki, Yoshikazu Yasuhiko, some sheet music, staff credits for the films and a brief discography of Joe's early solo works and Animage works.

There is a printing error on the tracklist insert for the Nausicaä Soundtrack.  It states track 13 is 虫愛ずる姫 and every track from 4 ~ 12 is shifted down one position.  This is incorrect and was independently verified against other Nausicaa Soundtracks.  Coincidentally, the tracklist printed on the cd itself is correct.
