Notes:
• D3-15~20 are the new tracks added to the PS4 version (Append Music Collection), D3-21~22 are unused tracks, D3-23~26 are jingles.
• Kosuke.F appeared on the PS4 port and only performs for Singa. This means he could have only performed D3-15, the only new track that uses real guitar performance.
• D3-23 was originally composed for Ys Eternal (1997), its real composer remains still unknown.
• D3-26 was originally composed by Yuzo Koshiro for Ys I.
