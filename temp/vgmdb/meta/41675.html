Notes

OverClocked ReMix Presents:
The Wily Castle ReMix Gauntlet 2011
http://ocremix.org/forums/showthread.php?t=37157

Director: Shariq 'DarkeSword' Ansari

The Wily Castle ReMix Gauntlet was a team-based Mega Man music remix competition run in the Fall of 2011. 12 teams competed every week to combine one of their chosen Robot Master themes with a provided Wily Castle theme. This is an archival release of all the entries sent in during the entirety of the competition.

Album art was drawn by OC ReMix forum member rnn and edited by DarkeSword.

This release is free and not-for-profit. Mega Man is copyright Capcom.

Check out OverClocked ReMix for more great remixes of all your favorite video games.
http://ocremix.org/
