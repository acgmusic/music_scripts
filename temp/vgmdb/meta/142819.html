Notes

Bungie Store version includes an in-game "Negative Harmony" emblem, and comes in MP3, Apple Lossless and FLAC formats.

-----

Every journey has a beginning. Yours began in service to the light; a single step that transformed into a brilliant adventure filled with cherished allies and impossible victories against Despots, Kells and literal Gods.

That same journey called you into darkness, and to a moment in time shaped by a terrifying force that promises an end to all things. But you do not-and will never-stand alone. When you listen to this music, you will hear us sharing your Triumphs and Defeats.

This was written for you, Guardian. It tells of your legend.

So begin here, with the sound of the Traveler’s light. Carry it with you into the shadow of the valley, as the path darkens and the threatens your resolve. Let it warm you and your allies as you stand against the Witness. We will stand with you and your victory will echo in our music.

Thank you for listening and for letting us join you on this 10-year journey.
