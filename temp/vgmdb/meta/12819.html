Notes

All music composed by Riei Saito.

Song of the Moon
Vocals: Miku Yoshikawa
Lyricist: NBGI (Tomomi Tagawa)
Composer: tri-Crescendo (Riei Saito)

The 2 title/ending vocal tracks of the game "Hikari" (光 / Light) and "Tsuki no Nukumori" (月のぬくもり / The Warmth of the Moon) performed by Aoi Teshima are not part of this release.
Those tracks are however available on the pre-order bonus CD FRAGILE DREAMS ~FAREWELL RUINS OF THE MOON~ MOONLIGHT TRACKS, the Hikari / Tsuki-no-Nukumori digital single of the game, or the "Collection Blue" (YCCW-10162) album.
