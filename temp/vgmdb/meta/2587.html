A softcover book with an original story by Masato Kato called "Five Seasons of kiЯitɘ" is included with the CD (the story and CD are meant to complement each other).

The first edition of this album includes the piano score to "Circle of Eternity", along with a message from Yasunori Mitsuda.

Despite the catalog number, this album contains only one disc. SBPS-0008 is the catalog number for the album, while SBPS-0009 is the catalog number for the book.

On the album "Kirite" is actually written with the (upper case) letter "R" and (lower case) letter "e" mirrored.

Additional catalog number: TGCS-2703
