Notes

The long-awaited sequel to last year's album, The Sonic Stadium Music Adventure 2012 is a community project put together by the creative and extremely talented folk of the SSMB. Showcasing the combined effort of over 40 different artists, this 8 hour behemoth of a compilation provides a wide variety of differing sounds from all seven corners of the world! With remixes paying tribute to some of the most renowned and obscure tunes in SEGA history, as well as a number of original compositions, this album is one for the AGES!

Sit back, relax, and enjoy the ride!

~ VizardJeffhog, Founder and Director of The Sonic Stadium Music Adventure series
