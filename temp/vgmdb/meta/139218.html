Notes

For our ninth mixtape, we asked our members to arrange their favorite opening or start menu themes. Our members responded by coming up with a number of different inventive arrangements. We hope you're ready to Press VVGO with us!

The team behind VVGO has decided to put the power to make music in the hands of our community! We open the door for smaller-scale pieces and a larger volume of pieces to manifest.
