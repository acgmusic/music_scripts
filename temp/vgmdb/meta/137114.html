Notes

Virtual Video Game Orchestra is proud to present our sixteenth project, "Opening ～ Bombing Mission" from Final Fantasy VII, along with end credits arrangement "Victory Fanfare" also from Final Fantasy VII! Together, these two performances feature 616 submissions on 88 unique instruments from 104 individual players across the global video game music community.

"OPENING ～ BOMBING MISSION"
in celebration of the 25th anniversary of Final Fantasy VII (1997)
based on music from Final Fantasy VII and Final Fantasy VII Remake (2020)
Music by Nobuo Uematsu (植松 伸夫)
Arranged by Brandon Harnish, Thomas Håkanson, Eddie Chung, Jonathan Shaw, Sophia W (herb)

"VICTORY FANFARE"
from Final Fantasy VII (1997)
Music by Nobuo Uematsu (植松 伸夫)
Arranged by Brandon Harnish, John Hovig Tabakian, Thomas Håkanson
