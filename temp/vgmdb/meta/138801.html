Notes

Triple Anniversary Album: 35th Anniversary of the Super Sentai Series + 30th Anniversary of the Space Sheriff Series + 40th Anniversary of the Chumei's Sounds

A selection album that condenses the essence of "Chumei Watanabe's Sounds".
It contains a total of 65 tracks from Super Sentai series and Space Sheriff series composed by Chumei Watanabe with the latest digital remastering, and a 28-page booklet is included.
