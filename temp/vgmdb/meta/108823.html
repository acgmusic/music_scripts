Notes

M01~33: Departure Melodies
M34~39: Sound Effects
M40~59: Automatic Broadcasting

The station in M16 and 41, Shinzushi (新逗子駅), has since been renamed Zushi·Hayama (逗子・葉山駅).
The station in M17, Haneda Airport (羽田空港駅), has since been renamed Haneda Airport Terminal 1·2 Station (羽田空港第1・第2ターミナル駅).

M01, 17, and 30 are arrangements of the song 'Red Train' (赤い電車) by Quruli.
M02 and 29 are arrangements of the song 'A Variety Of Lives' (人生いろいろ) by Chiyoko Shimakura.
M03 and 28 are arrangements of the minstrel song 'Camptown Races', originally written by Stephen Foster.
M04 and 27 are arrangements of the song 'Isn’t It A Good Hot Bath' (いい湯だな) by Duke Aces.
M05 and 26 are arrangements of the song 'If We Could Meet In Our Dreams' (夢で逢えたら) by Rats & Star, originally by Eiichi Ohtaki.
M06 and 25 are arrangements of the song 'I Look Up as I Walk' (上を向いて歩こう) by Kyu Sakamoto.
M07 and 24 are arrangements of the song 'Blue Light Yokohama' by Ayumi Ishida.
M08 and 23 are arrangements of the song 'Summer Color' (夏色) by Yuzu.
M09 and 22 are arrangements of the song 'MY HOME TOWN' by Kazumasa Oda.
M10 and 21 are arrangements of the song 'Road' (道) by EXILE.
M11 and 20 are arrangements of the song 'Yokosuka Story' (横須賀ストーリー) by Momoe Yamaguchi.
M12 and 19 arrangements of the song 'The Day The Gull Flew' (かもめが翔んだ日) by Machiko Watanabe.
M13 and 15 are arrangements of the song 'Cosmos' (秋桜) by Momoe Yamaguchi.
M14 is an arrangement of the song 'Cape Circumference' (岬めぐり) by Koutaro Yamamoto.
M16 is an arrangement of the song 'LIFE' by Kimaguren.
M18 is an arrangement of the theme from the movie 'Godzilla', originally composed by Akira Ifukube.
M32 is an arrangement of the movement '6 Ländlerische Tänze, K.606 No. 1' by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart.
