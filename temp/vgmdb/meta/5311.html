Notes

SUPER RPG SCOOP COLLECTION: 01~14

01: Torneko no Daibouken: Fushigi no Dungeon [Chunsoft]
Composer: Koichi Sugiyama

02~03: Mystic Quest [Square]
Composer: Ryuji Sasai (2), Yasuhiro Kawakami (3)

04~05: Ranma 1/2: Akanekodan-teki Hihou [Shogakukan Productions, Toho]
Composer: Toshio Okamoto

06~08: Aretha [Yanoman]
Composer: Koji Abe
Synthesizer version arranger uncredited

09~10: Aqutallion (Secret of the Stars) [Tecmo Soft Products]
Composer: Tsukushi Sasaki, Chinako

11~14: Monster Maker 3 ~Hikari no Majutsushi~ [SOFEL]
Composer: T's Music (11), CUBE (12~14)
Synthesizer version arranger uncredited (11, 12)
Miyoko Takaoka (CUBE) has included this game in a works list, but it's likely that there were other composers as well.


NEW GAME SOUND MUSEUM: 15~40

15~19: New Japan Pro-Wrestling: Chou Senshi IN Tokyo Dome [Varie]
Composer: HAMASAN
Synthesizer version arranger uncredited

20~21: Thunderbirds: Kokusai Kyuujotai Shutsudou Seyo! [Cobra Team]
Composer: Kennosuke Suemura
Despite not being marked as synthesizer versions, these tracks are also arranged by an uncredited arranger

22~24: Pretty Soldier Sailormoon [Angel]
Composer: Takanori Arisawa

25~26: Winning Post [KOEI]
Composer: Youichi Yamazaki

27~29: Jutei Senki [Enix]
Composer: TAM-TAM
Music composed by Kiyotaka Sato, Tsutomu Shimizu, & Nobuhiro Makino
Track 27 composed by Kiyotaka Sato & Tsutomu Shimizu. Specific composers for the other tracks unknown.

30~32: DESTRUCTIVE (Bazooka Blitzkrieg) [Bandai]
Composer: STUDIO401 (Uchu-Jin)

33~35: SD Kidou Senshi Gundam 2 [Angel]
Composer: Studio Kumataro (Yuki Sabakuma), PRO-MARS, Hi-Tech-Icchy

36~40: Mouse-senyou Mario to Wario (Mario & Wario) [Nintendo]
Composer: Junichi Masuda
