Notes

Relive the magical journey through space and time as GameGrooves pays tribute to Yasunori Mitsuda's iconic soundtrack. Whether you're enjoying the chilled-out beats of "Arni (Home World)" or jamming to the post-hardcore "The Girl Who Stole the Stars," you're sure to be dreaming of another world while you listen to the 16 covers on this album.
