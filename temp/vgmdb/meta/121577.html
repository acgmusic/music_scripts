Notes

Reprint edition for "Tetsuwan Atom Series" records individually released in 1965 with theme song single. It includes 5 card with original jackets drawings of original records more 2 cards for theme song single record and a sticker. Available on analog record box (CRJ-1003~7)

Disc 1 (01,02), reprint of LW-4021-EM released in Feb 01, 1965
Disc 1 (03,04), reprint of LW-4027-EM released in Mar 01, 1965
Disc 1 (05); Disc 2 (01), reprint of LW-4028-EM released in May 01, 1965
Disc 2 (02,03), reprint of LW-4034-EM released in Jun 01, 1965
Disc 2 (04,05), reprint of LW-4035-EM released in Jul 01, 1965

Fairy tale drama based on the monthly magazine "Kobunsha Kappa Comics Astro Boy":
- No. 6 (01 June 1964) with original jacket drawings [Card 1]
- No. 9 (01 September 1964) with original jacket drawings [Card 2]
- No. 12 (01 December 1964) with original jacket drawings [Card 3]
- No. 10 (01 October 1964) with new cover drawings [Card 4]
- No. 4 (01 April 1964) with new cover drawings [Card 5]

All the dramas are edited versions of the TV soundtrack:
- Disc 1 (01,02) from episodes 14
- Disc 1 (03,04) from episode 37
- Disc 1 (05); Disc 2 (01) from episode 32
- Disc 2 (02,03) from episode 28
- Disc 2 (04,05) from episode 6
Kyoji Kobayashi's expressive narratives are included. Record planner work by Nobu Hiroshi is his last in the Crown art era.
