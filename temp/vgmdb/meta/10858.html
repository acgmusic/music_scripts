Notes

The Legendary Edition includes the "Echoes of War" DVD, while the standard edition does not.


Disc 1:

01-02: The Third War Symphony (Warcraft III)
03-05: The Shadow of the Legion Symphony (World of Warcraft and World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade)
06: The Visions of the Lich King (World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King)

Disc 2:

01-04: The Koprulu Symphony (StarCraft and StarCraft: Ghost)
05: The Hyperion Overture (StarCraft II)
06-08: The Symphony of Sanctuary (Diablo, Diablo II, Diablo II: Lord of Destruction and Diablo III)
