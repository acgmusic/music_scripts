Notes

Note: This album was made-to-order and was only available from September 2 to October 31, 2022.

In addition to the CDs, this release includes a deluxe booklet, the orchestra score of the series main theme, and the piano score book for Bungo and Alchemist Piano Solo Collection (including the new tracks from this release).


● Bungo and Alchemist Stage Play Series Music Collection (2 CD)
- Exclusive to this release
- Includes music from the five stage plays for the series:
  1. Bungo and Alchemist: Yokeimono no Elegy
  2. Bungo and Alchemist: Itansha no Waltz
  3. Bungo and Alchemist: Tsuzuribito no Canon
  4. Bungo and Alchemist: Hinekuremono no Aria
  5. Bungo and Alchemist: Nagekibito no Rondo

● Bungo and Alchemist × OEK Kanazawa Concert（1 CD）
- Exclusive to this release
- Includes recordings from the concert performed by Orchestra Ensemble Kanazawa in October 2021

● Bungo and Alchemist Music Collection (1 CD)
- Exclusive to this release
- Includes music from the original album release with the addition of 7 previously unreleased tracks

● Bungo and Alchemist Piano Solo Collection (1 CD)
- Exclusive to this release
- Includes music from the original album release with the addition of 3 previously unreleased tracks

● Bungo and Alchemist Orchestra Concert (1 CD)
- Remastered version of the original album release

● Bungo to Alchemist ~Shinpan no Haguruma~ Music Collection (2 CD)
- Remastered version of the original album release
