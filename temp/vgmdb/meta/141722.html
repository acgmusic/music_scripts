Notes

The adventure of STRAY GODS continues with the Orpheus! An absolute honor to return to David Gaider / Summerfall's playground, and doubly so to spend so much time working with the illustrious Anthony Rapp.

Big shoutout to my cowriters Simon Hall, David Gaider, Montaigne and joining the crew for the first time, the hilarious Tom Hardy!
