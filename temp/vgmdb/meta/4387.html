Notes

Original music from two Konami MSX2 games: the famous cyberpunk adventure "SNATCHER" and its comedic RPG retelling "SD SNATCHER".

DISC1: SNATCHER
Music Compose: Masanori Adachi, Seiichi Fukami, Masahiro Ikariko, Mutsuhiko Izumi, S. Masuda, Iku Mizutani, M. Shirakawa
Naming: Naoki Matsui, Hideo Kojima

(Track 6 is an arrangement of 'Jingle Bells', originally composed by James Lord Pierpont.)

DISCS 2&3: SD SNATCHER
Music Compose: Masahiro Ikariko, Mutsuhiko Izumi, Motoaki Furukawa, Michiru Yamane, Yuji Takenouchi, Harumi Ueko, Yuko Kurahashi, Tomoya Tomita, Tsuyoshi Sekito, Kazuhiko Uehara, Mr. Inoue
