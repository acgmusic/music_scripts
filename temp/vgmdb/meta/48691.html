Notes

"This compact disc, includes music of Lupin The 3rd used in the first T.V. series. we tried to restore the music to it's original condition but since the original tapes have gone long time ago, we had no choice but copying from M.E. (Music & Effects) tapes. Consequently, you will hear sound effects in the music (cars, guns and actions, etc...) and we apologize that."

Music & Whistled by TAKEO YAMASHITA
Vocal & Scat

KAYOKO ISHU=08,11,13,23,24,25,30,44
YOSHIRO HIROISHI=26 (uncredited on booklet, is wrongly credited to CHARIE CORSEY)
Track 17,26,50=Licensed by Nippon Colombia Co., Ltd.
Lyrics: TOKYO MOVIE KIKAKUBU (uncredited on booklet)


*notes from booklet:
Old Lupin master tapes were lost, restoration work from ME tapes of all 23 episodes (sound material tapes with only music and sound effects selected according to the scene, without dialogue), which are sound materials created in the production process of the work. There is a difference in the level of sound (loudness) in the parts where dialogue is inserted.

■Lupin Sansei Theme Song I■ opening theme (episodes 1~3)
Recorded as "Lupin III Part 2" from single KU-620, title was changed later by Nippon Columbia as "Lupin III Part 1". Koji Makaino is the arranger for record version by Teichiku Records but is name was deleted after record exclusive distribution rights went to Nippon Columbia, so Takeo Yamashita was wrongly credited as the arranger. It is speculated that Takeo Yamashita was the arranger for TV original size as first version produced.

■Lupin Sansei Theme Song II■ ending theme (all episodes)
(50) from episode 3, motorcycle SE before the song. Recorded as "Lupin III Part 1" from single KU-620, title was changed later by Nippon Columbia as "Lupin III Part 2". Koji Makaino is the arranger for record version by Teichiku Records.

■AFRO"LUPIN'68"■ opening theme (episodes 4,5,7~15)
(09) instrumental from episode 6 with narration by Yasuo Yamada, other episodes used COCC-9081~5 version
(17) TV original from episodes 14 and 15th, compared COCC-14667~8 version has been edited, shorter few seconds.

■Lupin III Theme Song 3■ opening theme (episodes 17~23)
(26) from episode 16 with narration by Goro Naya, missing in COCC-14667~8 and COCC-9081~5. Episode 12th used another short version, not published in this album.
