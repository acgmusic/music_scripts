Notes

As part of the 45th anniversary of the creation of "Goldorak" (aka "UFO Robot Grendizer" in original version), in agreement with Dynamic Planning and Toei Animation, PRIME PROD has decided to offer several events that invite you to rediscover one of the greatest icons of Pop Culture, while celebrating the strong cultural link between France and Japan: it's the retrospective "Goldorak XperienZ".

This album contains the recording of the live at the Grand Rex in Paris closing the "Goldorak XperienZ" exhibition. The concert includes the music from the series "Goldorak" performed by a symphony orchestra, as well as various French opening and ending themes from TV anime performed by the original singers.
