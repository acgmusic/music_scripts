Notes

On our sixth mixtape, we invited our members to think of their favorite character themes. Our members responded by coming up with a number of different inventive arrangements. We hope you enjoy our Character Select mixtape!

The team behind VVGO has decided to put the power to make music in the hands of our community! We open the door for smaller-scale pieces and a larger volume of pieces to manifest.

Album Art: Troisnyx
2D Artist: Grace Fiacco, Lyndsi Gutierrez, Tamaira Jaszczak, Jordy de Lat, Elliot McAuley
