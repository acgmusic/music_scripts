Notes

2LP on clear and orange translucent vinyl.

It will feature artwork from the SNK archives by the legendary illustrator Shinkiro, in addition to in-depth liner notes by Greg Kasavin of Supergiant Games and development interviews with SNK members Yasuyuki Oda, Youichiro Soeda and Nobuyuki Kuroki.

The first 150 copies are limited and include a certificate of authenticity signed by Yasuyuki Oda and Youichiro Soeda. The record itself however is the same as the regular version.

Notes from Parent Album:
"ART OF FIGHTING 2 The Definitive Soundtrack features all 34 tracks from the original 1994 NEO GEO title, remastered and restored to the best possible quality."
