Contains the original soundtrack of "Rakugaki Kingdom 2," the sequel to "Magic Pengel: The Quest for Color."

Hako no Niwa means Box Garden or Garden in a Box. The name of track 10 contains three kanji separated by dots which usually read 'saboten' (cactus). The individual kanji however read 'Sen-nin-shou' (or Hermit's Palm), which is an older reading for the same word.
