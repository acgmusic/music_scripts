Notes

A preorder bonus DVD of the game containing:
1) audio Drama (45 mins in total)
2) a password to unlock a guest character (Rona Murasame from Shining Hearts).
3) all the 50 tracks composed by Hiroki Kikuta
4) character songs
5) visual collection
6) special card for a collectable card game
7) digital material (character voice, wallpapers and icons)
