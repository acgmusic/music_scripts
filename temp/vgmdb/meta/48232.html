Notes

Original soundtrack collection from Studio Ghibli includes nine soundtrack CDs for animation films directed by Isao Takahata. These CDs have been remastered in the HQCD format for the best sound and are fully compatible with standard CD players. Their covers are cardboard sleeves faithfully replicating the original LP cover artworks. A tenth bonus CD is a recording of the Tale of Princess Kaguya soundtrack played using traditional Japanese musical instruments. Also included is a catalog booklet.
