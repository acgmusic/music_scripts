Notes

This album was recalled due to the use of Islamic vocal samples in track 51. Sales of the enclosure was halted on Nov 22, 2019 and an updated version of the enclosure was shipped starting from Dec 16, 2019. Customers who had already purchased the enclosure were offered the opportunity to exchange the bonus disc for the corrected version.
