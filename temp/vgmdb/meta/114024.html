Notes

Sampler CD given out at Tokyo Game Show 2019 to those who tried the demo to Atelier Ryza: Ever Darkness & the Secret Hideout, received Gust-chan and Kogust-chan business cards for doing so, and presented them at Gust's sales booth when making a purchase.
