Notes

Welcome Home: A Chamber Tribute to Hometown Themes is a 14-track album filled with cozy chamber covers that'll bring you back home after a long journey just in time to wind down and settle in for the year's end. Grab some hot cocoa and feel the gentle cool breeze as you listen to these relaxing hometown themes.
