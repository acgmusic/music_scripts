Notes

Compilation album containing the songs related to the animation works of Leiji Matsumoto, who celebrated the 50th anniversary of his career in 2003.
It includes a lot of songs from the album "Matsumoto Leiji no Sekai" (CC-4341~2) but in a different order, adding songs from new released anime like "Queen Emeraldas" and "Submarine Super 99".
The song "Watashitachi no Mirai (Piano Version)" is also released on CD for the first time.
41 STEREO tracks in total.
