Notes

The limited first print of VOL.1 included a box to house both VOL.1 and VOL.2.

- "Bonus Track 1" contains the Space Harrier theme from the Japanese Master System BIOS. This is the only Master System tune that utilises both the PSG and FM sound synthesisers. It also contains the Sega soundbite that has been in use since the days of the SG-1000.
- "Bonus Track 2" contains the music from the Japanese Mega CD BIOS.

"LAGHTER" and "THE PROMISSING FUTURE" are actual titles from the tracklist.
