This Compilation including 33 Tracks. Theme Songs on TV Soundtracks in 1971 and Special Session in 1980 with Single Records Tracks. These Tracks Made by Takeo Yamashita with a Wild Sensibility for Music!

■TV size Theme Collection■
Vocal by: Charley Corsey (01~04,09~12)
              Yoshiro Hiroishi (06~08) first time credited!
■Single Records version■
Vocal by: Charley Corsey (13,14)
■Album [Lupin The Third TV Original BGM Collection Music From The Original Motion Picture Soundtrack Score]■
Original Record, CQ-7040

Composed and conducted by Takeo Yamashita

*more infos from liner notes:
01. same sound source as [Lupin III Original Soundtrack Drama Hen, CZ-7032]
02. without motorbike sound effect
03. with motorbike sound effect at the beginning but not at the end, same sound source as [Lupin The 3rd '71 ME TRACKS, VPCD-81271]
04. narration by Yasuo Yamada, same sound source as [Lupin III Original Soundtrack Drama Hen, CZ-7032], [LUPIN THE THIRD BOX Part II, COCC-9081~5 -Disc 5 track 25]. It's called for convenience as "Lupin Part 4"
05. alternative narration by Yasuo Yamada, same sound source as [Lupin The 3rd '71 ME TRACKS, VPCD-81271]
06-07. narration by Goro Naya, same sound source as [Lupin III Original Soundtrack Drama Hen, CZ-7032], but not as [LUPIN THE THIRD BOX Part II, COCC-9081~5] and [LUPIN THE THIRD Theme History, COCC-14667~8]
08. same sound source as [LUPIN THE THIRD BOX Part II, COCC-9081~5], [LUPIN THE THIRD Theme History, COCC-14667~8] and [LUPIN THE BEST! PUNCH THE ORIGINALS!, COCP-50668]
09. with motorbike sound effect at the end
10-11. from [TV Size! TV Manga Shudaika no Ayumi 2, COCX-31668] first time recording. Sound effect was recorded in the second half, and a percussion prior to dubbing
12. same sound source as [LUPIN THE THIRD Theme History, COCC-14667~8]
13.14. ending theme originally called "Lupin III Part 1" by Union/Teichiku record was later changed by Nippon Columbia to "Lupin III Part 2"; opening theme originally "Lupin III Part 2" changed to "Lupin III Part 1".
First time after original Teichiku record [KU-620] Koji Makaino has been linked as arranger of "Lupin III Part 1&2" record version. For unknown reasons his name was deleted by Nippon Columbia in every Lupin III album ever released until this.
Until 1998 "Lupin Part" songs weren't registered in JASRAC, so Yomiuri TV registered them as we know now rather "Lupin Theme Song".
