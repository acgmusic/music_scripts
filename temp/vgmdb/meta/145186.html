Notes

Distributed at the 2004 Summer in Animate AV Fair.
It was made available for those who accrued a certain amount of points in a scratch card contest, which was held at Animate locations from July 16 (Fri) to August 31 (Tue) of that year.
Said points were able to be redeemed from the start of the event until September 5 (Sun).

Each song on the album was performed by voice actors playing the roles of fictional Animate store managers.
