Notes

2LP vinyl release on 180g vinyl.
First pressing comes on blue and white colored vinyl, while all later pressings are on black vinyl.

Includes booklet with interview with Jun Senoue and Takashi Iizuka as well as liner notes by John Linneman, song lyrics and character art. Also includes at digital download for Bandcamp allowing you to download the album in most traditional audio formats in both lossy and lossless (16bit/44.1khz)
