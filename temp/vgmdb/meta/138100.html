Notes

"BE-MUSIC Special Selection 2020 is finally ready!
We've compiled music from the following events from 2020:
- Be-Music West II -
- PABAT! 2020 seasons -mint world feat. chocolate-
- SPRING 9KEYS BLOSSOM 2020
- Mutual Faith 3
-WORLD WAR - BATTLE ROYALE IN ALL REGIONS -

I was surprised and happy to see the very high acceptance rate this time (a little over 75% of songs I contacted people about)! Thank you!
Of course, that also means the compilation is huge. 2020 had some really good BMS, so I hope people find the songs they like in here. Once again I'm including links on the website to the original BMS, so people who haven't played them can check them out easily.
This time, 140/fth handled the graphic design and album art, so everything looks nice and shiny too!
That's about all. Thank you to everyone who contributed or helped out!"
