Notes

2LP splatter vinyl release made available through a successful campaign on Bandcamp's vinyl crowdfunding service.

Notes from Parent Album:
"Approximately ten years since the release of their landmark collaboration album, Mega Ran and K-Murdock return one final time to commemorate the moment that is "Forever Famicom."
This serves as a special re-release, as every track was mixed again, remastered with EVERY in-game sample meticulously and pain-stakingly replayed by the talented pianist Rob "88bit" Kovacs.
Enjoy Mega Ran's storytelling over a fresh new coat of paint as we return to the colorful times of 8 and 16-bit!"
