Notes

The 2nd soundtrack to be produced & released containing arranged BGM found exclusively on the 1996 console version of TEKKEN 2.
This second volume contains half of the arranged BGM for TEKKEN 2 on the Sony PlayStation. Soundtrack includes (AC) opening movie, character stages & ending BGM. It also includes a number of themes from the previous game, "TEKKEN", which were recycled and assigned to various sub-boss characters for the Sony PlayStation version of TEKKEN 2.
