Notes

Contains a recording of the "Tentalive" concert held on January 26th, 2019 at Tokaigi 2019 and the "Shiokalive" concert held on November 3rd, 2016 at niconico Super Party 2016, as well as music from the game not included on the original soundtrack release.

The limited edition Blu-ray contains a video recording of the concert.
