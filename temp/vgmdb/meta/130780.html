Notes

Virtual Video Game Orchestra is proud to present our eighteenth project, "Explorers of Sky" from Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky, along with end credits arrangement "Top Menu Theme" also from Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky! Together, these two performances feature 190 submissions on 45 unique instruments from 64 individual players across the global video game music community.

"EXPLORERS OF SKY: A POKEMON MYSTERY DUNGEON MEDLEY"
from Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky (2009)
Music by Arata Iiyoshi (飯吉 新), Hideki Sakamoto (坂本 英城), Keisuke Ito (伊藤恵介)
Arranged by Olive Edwards
Additional Arrangement by Jonathan Shaw & Sophia W (Herb)

"TOP MENU THEME"
from Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky (2009)
Music by Arata Iiyoshi (飯吉 新), Hideki Sakamoto (坂本 英城), Keisuke Ito (伊藤恵介)
Arranged by Olive Edwards & Jonathan Shaw
