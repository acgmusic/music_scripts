Vocals on track 21 by Jacquelyn Breckenridge

The lyrics can be heard when played in reverse. The text originates from the 18th century children's bedtime prayer called "Now I Lay Me Down to Sleep".

When played in reverse, the lyrics are:

"I pray the lord (I pray the lord) my soul to keep,
(If I should die) If I should die before I wake,
I pray the lord (I pray the lord) my soul to take"
