Notes

I've been asked to put up download links on quite a few of my remixes and so I decided to make a best of album instead. All tracks are remixes that I did in 2010.

- Track information -

01. Alex Kidd in the Enchanted Castle - Desert Remix

Source(s): Scorpion Desert / Pyramid
Game: Alex Kidd in the Enchanted Castle
Composer(s): Tarnya

Hyde209 notes: I've wanted to remix this song for some time now.
Simply beacause it's a SEGA classic.
Also I wanted to play something joyful for a change.
In this remix I used my girlfriend's grandpa's old accordion.
I'm not much of an accordion player. It sure is fun to play though ^^

02. Blaster Master - Blast Away Remix

Source(s): ???
Game: Blaster Master
Composer(s): Naoki Kodaka

Hyde209 notes: I wanted to make a different remix so I made this slightly disco like remix.
The game is Blaster Master on the NES and it's a great game.

03. Shadow of the Ninja - Burning City Remix

Source(s): Burning City
Game: Shadow of the Ninja
Composer(s): Iku Mizutani & Kouichi Yamanishi

Hyde209 notes: This is my remix of level 5 from Blue Shadow also known as Shadow of the Ninja.
This is one of my favorite NES games. A game packed with Ninjas, huge morter spitting tanks and aswome music.

04. CastleVania Chronicles - The Tower of Dolls Remix

Source(s): Tower of Dolls
Game: Castlevania Chronicles
Composer(s): Shin Chan, Jigokuguruma Nakamura (Keizo Nakamura) & Devi Kobayashi (Hiroshi Kobayashi)

Hyde209 notes: Most castlevania games have great music. This game is no exception.
I chose to remix this song because is has a really nice piano track.
A great piano can make a song so much better.

05. DuckTales 2 - Egypt Remix

Source(s): Egypt
Game: Duck Tales 2
Composer(s): ???

Hyde209 notes: I really like the feeling of oriental music. I came up with the idea to make this remix when I got my hands on a nice Darbuka plug for VST. Some Mr Bungle tracks contributed with inspiration too.

06. James Pond - License to Bubble Remix

Source(s): License to Bubble
Game: James Pond: Underwater Agent
Composer(s): Richard Joseph, arranged for the Sega Mega Drive by Michael Barlow

Hyde209 notes: This tune was a nice break from everything complicated.
Just a clean rock 'n roll remix a la 60s/70s.
I love that sound of the Wurlitzer!

07. Kabuki Quantum Fighter - Level 1 Remix

Source(s): Level 1
Game: Kabuki Quantum Fighter
Composer(s): Masaki Hashimoto & Takahiro Wakuta

Hyde209 notes: This is a great NES game with some cool but strange music.
After my Megaman 3 remix I started to dig in my NES music library,
Out of the blue came this funky song and I just had to remix it.

08. Legend of Galahad - World 3 Remix

Source(s): World 3
Game: The Legend of Galahad
Composer(s): Matt Furniss

Hyde209 notes: I'm a huge fan of Matt Furniss and his game music. I tried to stay true to the original with this remix cause the tune is too great to spoil in any way. I worked a litte longer with this remix than I usually do. I just had to make it sound right.

09. Legend of Zelda - Labyrinth Remix

Source(s): ???
Game: The Legend of Zelda
Composer(s): Koji Kondo

Hyde209 notes: I made this remix a few days ago but I haven't benn able to upload it here untill now. Something was tragen with the video uploader, but now I'm back.
This is a remix of a true 8-Bit classic. Legend of Zelda.

10. Mega Man 3 - Gemini Man Remix

Source(s): Geminiman Stage
Game: Mega Man 3
Composer(s): Bunbun & Harumi Fujita

Hyde209 notes: After my Eternal Champions remixes I wanted to make a classic NES remix.
This is the fruit of my first NES work.

11. Mutant League Hockey - Title Remix

Source(s): Title Theme
Game: Mutant League Hockey
Composer(s): Russell Lieblich & Michael J. Sokyrka

Hyde209 notes: I had a blast playing this game with my brother when I was a kid.
The intro theme reminds me of Danzig for some strange reason.

12. Power Blade - Sector 6 Remix

Source(s): Sector 6: City
Game: Power Blade
Composer(s): ???

Hyde209 notes: I love this old NES game. The music is freak'n great. I wanted to make a heavy rock version with the same driving feeling as the original song.

13. Puggsy - Aztec Temple Remix

Source(s): Aztec Temple
Game: Puggsy
Composer(s): Matt Furniss

Hyde209 notes: Here is another remix of a tune written by one of my all time favorit composers Matt Furniss.
The rhythm was so funky that I had to ad some Wah guitar.

14. Road Rash - Title Remix

Source(s): Main Theme
Game: Road Rash
Composer(s): Rob Hubbard

Hyde209 notes: Another remix of a true SEGA 16-bit classics ^^
Rob Hubbards music in Road Rash is as groovy as ever.
I listen to this music so often so I thought I had to pay homage with a remix.
This time I had my good friend davadij record a blazing hot guitar solo to spice things up a bit.

15. Robin Hood - Persian Prison Remix

Source(s): ???
Game: Robin Hood: Prince of Thieves
Composer(s): Paul Webb

Hyde209 notes: I played around with some tunes from this game yesterday. I thought it was really fun to play along on drums to this tune so I just had to make a remix ^^

16. Section Z - Destroy the Balangool Remix

Source(s): ???
Game: Section Z
Composer(s): ???

Hyde209 notes: NES music is almost always good...And the music in NES games by CAPCOM is always great. This is just another example of a NES tune that I think need more love from the fans

17. Sonic 2 - Mysterious Cave Remix

Source(s): Mystic Cave Zone
Game: Sonic the Hedgehog 2
Composer(s): Masato Nakamura

Hyde209 notes: I've been wanting to make a remix of this song for soo long.
A spooky and at the same time funky tune. What more can you ask for =D

18. Streets of Rage 2 - In the Bar Remix

Source(s): In the Bar
Game: Streets of Rage 2
Composer(s): Yuzo Koshiro

Hyde209 notes: This is a remix as requested by cucollins12.
A nice swing'n lounge jazz tune. I really had a good time making this remix, however these jazz chords was about to drive me crazy

19. Strider 2 - Streets of Neo Hong Kong Remix

Source(s): ???
Game: Strider 2
Composer(s): Setsuo Yamamoto & Etsuko Yoneda

Hyde209 notes: This is a remix requested by cucollins12.
Strider Hiryu was my idol when I was a kid so I just had to do it ^^
Besides it's a great game with some nice music.

20. Super Metroid - Red Soil Remix

Source(s): ???
Game: Super Metroid
Composer(s): ???

Hyde209 notes: I was trying different drum grooves to this simple but awsome theme from Super Metroid. I friend of mine pointed out that this was his favorite tune from SM so I had to remix it


Notes obtained from Hyde209 YouTube channel.
