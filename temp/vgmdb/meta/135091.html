General thoughts behind each CD's name is written down here (quotes by XK2238): Disc 1: "The word "Seekers" refers to all players within the GTS series as a whole. With how rigorously the staff members worked behind the scenes to prepare, package, and present each and every iteration (save for a *certain* variant), it's not a stretch to say that GTS have established a grandeur most sought-after among a select few. Bask in the thrilling ambience of these hand-picked tracks from the later stages across recent GTS iterations to date."

Disc 2: "After COEGTS 2023's full lore video was released, in regards to the newly introduced lesser god characters (at least they're new to me), I knew I had to bring them into the grand scheme of things somehow. When Beaukani implied that they've stood guard for centuries on end, I couldn't help but wonder why... what was the point? It's as if all of them had been performing an elegy in unison for their deceased (...?) lord with all their might, only for the elegy to be for themselves in the end. How utterly tragic."

Disc 3: "The encounter of self-revelation... the lengths of which Tsubaki had to go through... it could very well have been longer than the tower itself (hence "summit," just in the opposite direction). Even if it wasn't the case, the whole tower crumbled down to the ground in the end, so everything within it--seemingly including all the lesser gods--was gone without trace. The "[DATA EXPUNGED]" treatment... ever so frightening."
