Official soundtrack to the first season of the hit Nickelodeon animated series.

The Legend of Korra animated series created by Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko

All music composed and produced by Jeremy Zuckerman

M-02, M-08, and M-13 produced by Jeremy Zuckerman and Chris Tedesco; performed by The Angel City Dixieland Band, led by Chris Tedesco

Dizi, Erhu, Matouqin, Suona, Xiao, and Zhonghu performed by Hong Wang

Trumpet performed by Andrew Prahlow

Alto Sax and Baritone Sax performed by Aran Tanchum

Additional production by Benjamin Wynn

Published by Music By Nickelodeon Inc., admin. by Sony/ATV Melody (BMI)

Executive in Charge of Music for Nickelodeon: Doug Cohn

VP Music for Nickelodeon: Colleen Fitzpatrick

Mastered by Mike Wells Mastering

Designed by Anthony DelGrego and Sandra Pieloch

Special Thanks to: Gretchen Brennison, Megan Casey, John Conroy, Kevin Ellman, Joel Fisher, Russell Hicks, Brandon Hoang, Jennifer Jankowski, Pam Kaufman, Adriana Lemus, Rich Magallanes, Shalina Mansharamani, Chandler Poling, Kristina Rodriguez, James Salerno, Richard Siegmeister, Ellie Vainshtok, Scott Van Horn, Kristen Yu-Um, and Cyma Zarghami.

"The music for The Legend of Korra is truly something special. It adds a layer of emotion that can't be achieved with story and art alone. This soundtrack wouldn't have happened without the tireless effort of Megan Casey at Nickelodeon, who went to bat time and time again on our behalf. Thank you, Megan! And of course, a huge helping of thanks and gratitude goes out to Jeremy Zuckerman, the multi-talented composer behind the music, and one-half of The Track Team. There was no challenge too daunting, no request too crazy, that Jeremy couldn't handle. Thank you, Jeremy, for always going above and beyond, and for sharing your music with the show and the world. Also. thanks to Benjamin Wynn, the other half of The Track Team, whose sound design is an essential part of the Korra audio experience. And thanks to all the fans over the years who not only asked for a soundtrack, but started online petitions demanding its release. We are grateful for your passion and dedication to the show and its music. We think it was worth the wait."
- Mike & Bryan

Jeremy Zuckerman would like to thank Alicia, Cady, Olivia, and Riley Zuckerman and Susan Miller. My boundless gratitude to all the fans who feel so deeply. This is for you!

Benjamin Wynn would like to thank Lara Howenstine, and all the fans for supporting us and the show!
