Notes

Image album based on 2 manga by Shizue Takanashi: "Sora-chan no Boushi" and "Umi-chan Donna Iro?"

The first limited release comes with two handkerchiefs for a total of 2900 Yen.

Manga published in Kodansya "Nakayosi"
