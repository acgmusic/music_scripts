Notes

Selection of 50 anime/tokusatsu/children/original songs written by Yuu Aku, from the Showa era. Features the character "Bojji" from the manga "Ranking of Kings" ("Ousama Ranking"), by Sosuke Toka, for the front cover.
Includes 12 remastered tracks released for the first time on CD (marked *).
