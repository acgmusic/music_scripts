Notes

This vinyl contains all the French opening themes of TV series sung by Jean-Paul Cesari, entirely remastered.
It includes 4 unreleased tracks including the long version of the "Case Closed" opening theme (05) and 3 new versions of "Nicky Larson"'s cult opening theme song (09, 14 & 18).
Limited edition of 300 copies.


All vocals by Jean-Paul Cesari,
except 18: duet with KICKBAN
