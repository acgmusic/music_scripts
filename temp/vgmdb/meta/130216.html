Notes

Original Soundtrack containing newly recorded BGM from the TV series "The Hangman 6" (1987), and Opening Themes and BGM from the previous series "The Hangman" (1980 to 1986).
Also includes 3 songs.


Music: Kei Wakakusa

Composer and Arranger: Kei Wakakusa (01~05, 08~11, 13)
01~05: BGM from "The Hangman 6"
08. "I・OPENING": Opening Theme from "The Hangman"

10. "III ・OPENING": Opening Theme from "Shin Hangman"
10. "STARDUST": BGM from "The Hangman II"
11. "IV・OPENING": Opening Theme from "The Hangman 4"
11. "BRICK AVENUE": BGM from "The Hangman 4"
11. "V・OPENING”: Opening Theme from "The Hangman V"
13: BGM from "The Hangman"
