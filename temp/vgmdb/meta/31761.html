Notes

New recording of the original Space Battleship Yamato opening theme. Tr1 is used as the 1st opening theme and tr2 as the 3rd ending theme for Space Battleship Yamato 2199.

Both songs originally composed by Hiroshi Miyagawa and newly arranged by Akira Miyagawa, with lyrics by Yuu Aku.
