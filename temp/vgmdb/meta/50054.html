Notes

Tracks 71~91 are bonus tracks, only available in the downloadable Bandcamp and/or Steam versions.

The Steam soundtrack has all 90 tracks, + "God's Call", which is exclusive to the Steam release.
The downloadable Bandcamp version has all 90 tracks, except "God's Call".
The online Bandcamp version only has the first 70 tracks + M71 "Live in Joy".

Every track, Bandcamp or Steam, has an original cover attached to the file, usually representing the context of the game. The scans represent the associated track number.

Track 71: Used in LISA the Joyful
Track 72~76: Unused
Tracks 77~89: Introductions of wrestlers ingame
