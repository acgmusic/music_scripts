Notes

Unlike previous Falcom Special Box sets, this one contains original music from various Falcom games which hadn't received a soundtrack release.

Disc 1: MUSIC From LORD MONARCH / Advanced LORD MONARCH
Disc 2: MUSIC From Brandish
Disc 3: MUSIC From Popful Mail
Disc 4: Ys IV / The Legend of Xanadu / Brandish II PRE RELEASE

All Music Composed by Falcom Sound Team J.D.K.

-DISC 4-
