Notes

Metal covers of French Opening Themes for many animes/cartoons from the 70s to the 90s.
All songs by Bernard Minet, who is the original singer in many cases (except 05,09,10,13,17).
The album also contains two metal covers of "Les Musclés", his former band as a drummer (15 & 16).
