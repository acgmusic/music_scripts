Notes

Virtual Video Game Orchestra is proud to present our 13th project, "False Knight" from Hollow Knight. Along with "End Card: White Palace" played during the credits, this project features 235 recording submissions from musicians across the global video game music community.

"False Knight"
from Hollow Knight (2017)
Composed by Christopher Larkin
Arranged by Vineeta Muthuraj, Thomas Håkanson, and Tommsey
Lyrics by Team Cherry
Translated into Latin by Jonathan Balcewicz

from Hollow Knight (2017)
Composed by Christopher Larkin
Arranged by Vineeta Muthuraj
