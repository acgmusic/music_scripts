Notes

A collection of songs from different games and sources remixed/revamped/covered by yours truly. This is another ever-growing album like 'Dustbin Dodo'; whenever new remixes and covers are made, they'll be added here.

The collection isn't necessarily in chronological order, meaning that there will be an insane quality increase or decrease at points. Instead, to the best of my personal memory, it's in order of personal exposure.
