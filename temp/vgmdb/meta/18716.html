Notes

First, and so far, only commercial album released under Yuko Ishibashi's name.

Track 5 is a rearranged version of "大切なことば" from Atelier Iris 3 Grand Phantasm.

Track 10 is a rearrangement of "絆～KIZNA" from the Ar tonelico II Hymmnos Concert Side Blue "Waterway ~ MIO".
