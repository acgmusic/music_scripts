Notes

Takeo Yamashita's second collection project. Many popular TV soundtracks from the 60's and 70's such as "Shichinin no Keiji" (first time on CD), "Lone Wolf", "Jikan Desu yo" (first time on CD), etc., from original sound sources. 1st print comes with a slipcase and a different design.
