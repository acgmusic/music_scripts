Notes

Enclosed with Cyber City Oedo 808 - Blu-ray+CD Soundtrack Collector's Edition. This release includes the "UK soundtrack" by Rory McFarlane.

Notes from Parent Album:
This soundtrack is exclusive to the british Manga Entertainment VHS release of the Cyber City Oedo 808 OVA.

The original japanese soundtrack was composed by Kazz Toyama, which is also featured in the North American release of the OVA.
