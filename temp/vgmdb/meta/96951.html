Notes

Limited edition. Comes packaged in a special box case.

Notes from Parent Album:
Two bonus tracks, tracks 12 and 13, for this album were released via a html link (http://cnt.kingrecords.co.jp/soundhorizon/roman/index.php). This html link asked listeners to provide an answer to a riddle provided in the booklet for the album to access the tracks. The details of these tracks are listed below:

12 yaneuraroman 2:49
13 truemessage 7:09

The tracks were included on "Chronology [2005-2010]" (KIZC-149~151) as "屋根裏物語" and "11文字の伝言 [Le vrai message]" respectively.
