The song "BRAVERY SOUL," intended to appear as track 1.29 before "THE TOWER UNSEALED," was accidentally left off of disc 1. An additional disc containing the song and extra material was later sent to everyone who purchased the album, and the soundtrack was temporarily pulled from sale.

Most of the songs on this album are demo and prototype music. Songs that were actually released in games, magazines, etc. are noted below.

All music composed by Yuzo Koshiro, except where otherwise noted.
