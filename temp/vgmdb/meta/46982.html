Notes

Music composed, arranged and conducted by Koichi Fujino (except M-22).

M-22 is an interpretation of the famous Russian folk song Калинка (Kalinka) written & composed by Ivan Larionov.
Vocals by The Red Army Kinshichou Squad (赤軍合唱団錦糸町分隊/Sekigun Gasshoutai Kinshichou Buntai).
