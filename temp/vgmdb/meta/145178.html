Notes

'SAVIOR' presents to you songs with more colorful atmosphere than ever before.
Enjoy popular tracks from MapleStory, reimagined with fresh arrangements, alongside serene melodies evoking tranquil deserts and the vibrant harmony of nature. Immerse yourself in the refined essence of Eastern music, tailored to your taste.

[Credits]

01. Lord's Castle

Composed & Arranged by Park Ji-hoon (박지훈)
Keyboard Park Ji-hoon (박지훈)

A track that depicts a noble lord's castle radiating elegance under the blazing desert sun.
The gentle combination of piano and strings in the introduction captures the mysterious beauty of Erimos, while the gradual rise into a grand orchestral climax paints the majesty of the castle itself.


02. Oasis in the Desert

Composed & Arranged by Lee Jong-hyuk (이종혁)
Keyboard Lee Jong-hyuk (이종혁)

A track that symbolizes Erimos during its peaceful days, marked by the clear flow of an oasis.
It also holds the heartfelt memories of Kali and her younger sister, Rasha.


03. Outskirts of Erimos

Composed & Arranged by Kim Han-sol (김한솔)
Keyboard Kim Han-sol (김한솔)

The theme of the borderlands and outskirts of the desert, contrasting with the grandeur of the castle.
The repetitive piano melody evokes the desolate wind blowing through the barren desert, while the strings emerging in the latter half hint at the looming shadow of tragedy soon to engulf Erimos.


04. Carnival of the Night

Composed & Arranged by Chae Hana (채하나)
Keyboard Chae Hana (채하나)

As Ypsilon reveals her true nature, the dark and somber melody conveys her unfathomable malice.
After the climax, the brass instruments that mysteriously conclude the piece symbolize the ominous and enigmatic power of the noble lord.


05. Realize Truth

Composed & Arranged by Shin Yushik (신유식)
Keyboard Shin Yushik (신유식)

When she finally realized who she was, she was confronted with the sacrifice of her loved ones.
The cruel truth of their fate, which had been predetermined since birth.
The short but intense quire emphasizes this tragedy even more dramatically.


06. The Land of Peach Blossoms

Composed & Arranged by Chae Hana (채하나)
Keyboard Chae Hana (채하나)

A tantalizing oriental breeze from a mystical pictorial paradise.
Listening to the sound of wind instruments,
and the fragrance of flowers and trees from the forests of the four seasons blowing in the clear breeze.


07. 사계의 갈림길 (Invasion)   [Literal Translation: The Crossroads of the Four Seasons]

Composed & Arranged by Shin Yushik (신유식)
Keyboard Shin Yushik (신유식)

Standing at the crossroads leading to the season that opposes her malice in order to stop Karine,  
The uneasy tones of strings and the violently shifting percussion convey the tension right before battle.


08. 파괴된 계절 (Destroyed Four Seasons)   [Literal Translation is also the same.]

Composed & Arranged by Shin Yushik (신유식)
Keyboard Shin Yushik (신유식)

A track that embodies a season that seems to have lost its vitality, using a minimalistic approach with a repetitive melody.
The fast-paced percussion session and low-toned choir depict the precarious battle against the massive malice, as it relentlessly unfolds.


09. 참혹의 겨울 (Faded Winter)   [Literal Translation: The Winter of Desolation]

Composed & Arranged by Shin Yushik (신유식)
Keyboard Shin Yushik (신유식)

As the name suggests, this track vividly portrays the harsh and unforgiving cold of winter.
The urgent, driving bass and sharp choir create a powerful atmosphere, reflecting Karin's overwhelming presence and the despairing mood as she absorbs the malice.


10. 죽음을 두른 사계 (Ruination of Four Seasons)   [Literal Translation: The Four Seasons of Death]

Composed & Arranged by ASTERIA (김가해)
Keyboard ASTERIA (김가해)

A fight unfolds in the midst of the four seasons that are completely collapsing due to the power of the outrageous Karing and the Four Evils.
The overwhelming volume of the orchestra is combined with the oriental elements that symbolize Karing,
and the fierce battle between her and her adversary unfolding in the midst of destruction is expressed in a desperate and thrilling way.


11. Four Auspicious Beasts

Composed & Arranged by Kim Han-sol (김한솔)
Keyboard Kim Han-sol (김한솔)

The peaceful and hopeful tune is like a downgradient that has finally regained its light.
The rich, colorful instrumentation and harmonies are like the four seasons singing their original colors.


12. Wonstaurant

Composed & Arranged by Chae Hana (채하나)
Keyboard Chae Hana (채하나)
Guitalele Sung Sangmin (성상민)

A charming Wongstaurant filled with lovingly prepared dishes made by Wonky and his adorable chef friends!  
The lively, bouncy rhythm evokes the bright atmosphere of the restaurant and the cheerful greetings from the characters, bringing the vibrant setting to life.


13. Floral Life - Xmas ver.

Composed by CODA SOUND
Arranged by Shin Yushik (신유식)
Keyboard Shin Yushik (신유식)

A joyful Christmas festival celebrated in Henesys!
The lively and cheerful theme of Henesys, Floral Life, is adorned with the merry sound of bells, announcing that winter has arrived throughout the village.


14. Leafre - Xmas ver.

Composed by Studio EIM
Arranged by Kim Han-sol (김한솔)
Keyboard Kim Han-sol (김한솔)
E.Guitar Sung Sangmin (성상민)

Christmas has arrived in the lively, nature-filled village of Leafre.
The sight of the halflings preparing a winter festival for the adventurers adds a touch of warmth and charm to the clear, serene forest.


15. The Lake of Oblivion - Xmas ver.

Composed by Chae Hana (채하나)
Arranged by Lee Jong-hyuk (이종혁)
Keyboard Lee Jong-hyuk (이종혁)

Even towns that are usually filled with loneliness and silence are filled with Christmas objects this winter.
Nothing fancy, but a heartwarming carol-like melody that warms the heart.
The unnamed village enjoys winter in a quiet and simple way.


16. Grand Athenaeum: The Other Side of the Dimension

Composed by Studio EIM
Arranged by Sung Sangmin (성상민)
Keyboard Sung Sangmin (성상민)

The theme of the dimensional library, which holds all the stories of the world, has been arranged more vividly and powerfully.  
The mysterious and clear tones reveal the grandeur of the library in its true form, and the narratives it houses seem to come alive, exuding a sense of vitality.
