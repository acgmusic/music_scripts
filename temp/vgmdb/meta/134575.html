Notes

Snow Stage is Speed Jammers's first independent video game music charity album jam, and we're very excited to share it with you! Almost 30 musicians arranged, recorded, and mixed this album in three days.

Speed Jammers started in 2023 out of the team that put together the VGM Con charity album jam. We wanted to continue what we saw as a fun way to bring VGM musicians together to collaborate on a community album. We have decided to focus our charitable efforts on benefiting organizations that directly impact the VGM community.

For Snow Stage, all proceeds raised by this album will go towards supporting Bonus Stage. Bonus Stage is a nonprofit society creating opportunities for video-game-inspired performance art and culture. Started in 2015 in Vancouver as an annual variety show, it has widely broadened its scope of events both on and offline, offering regular virtual concerts and listening parties for members of the VGM, nerdcore, and chiptune communities, as well as hosting concerts, burlesque shows and unique gaming experiences in Vancouver.

To find out more about Bonus Stage, please visit their website: www.bonusstage.ca
