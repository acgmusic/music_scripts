Notes

"Here is a compilation of various tracks of mine from 2015 to 2020! I included only my best works, and some of the earlier stuff has been polished up to meet my current standards. The last seven tracks are covers/remixes of tracks I didn't originally write, mostly tracks from games. There are also four bonus tracks for you to enjoy.

You'll find tracks in a variety of genres here, such as chiptune, chillout, techno, plunderphonics, jazz fusion, house, D&B and more. Enjoy!"
