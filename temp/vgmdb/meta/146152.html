Notes

A recording of Yuriko Nakamura's performance of the Rikuzentaka "miracle organ," which was sold at the Tokyo National Museum gift shop during an exhibition of said organ.
Once located at the Rikuzentakata city museum in Iwate Prefecture, it was damaged in the 2011 Tohoku earthquake and tsunami.
Work to restore the organ was completed in 2015, and this CD was produced in celebration of that accomplishment.
