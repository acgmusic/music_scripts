Notes

On this, our fifth mixtape, we invited our members to think of pieces that represented the best of video games in the 20th Century. Our members responded by coming up with a number of different inventive arrangements. We hope you enjoy our 20th Century Classics mixtape!

The team behind VVGO has decided to put the power to make music in the hands of our community! We open the door for smaller-scale pieces and a larger volume of pieces to manifest.
