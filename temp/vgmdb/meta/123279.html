Notes

VAGRANCY best vocal album in commemorating the circle's 20th anniversary. Contains 20 tracks in total, including 2 new tracks.

In 11th July 2022, the Akiko Shikata's Pixiv Fanbox site opened a survey (https://shikata-akiko.fanbox.cc/posts/4221052) for fans, who have subscribed to the account, to vote for which songs to remake.
