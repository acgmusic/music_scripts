A Message from Fox Amoore (aka lain Fox Armour)
"Jings, I'm not even sure where to begin. Originally this was going to be a shot-in-the-dark fundraiser to record a Piano album in STUDIO 2 at Abbey Road. Instead, I remember staring in awe at the computer screen when I see it gets funded in 2 hours, instead of the 60 days that I had originally planned. So the next day I happily adjusted the goal, which was to go all out and hire a world class Orchestra to perform some of my orchestral works. What a jaw-dropping and humbling experience that was... To sit up on the balcony with some of my closest friends in STUDIO 1, to then hear the English Chamber Orchestra start playing Dreaming of You. I cannot put into words what that felt like, however I hope through this album, you'll be able to not just hear, but FEEL just what it was like on those amazing days at Abbey Road Studios.
Come Find Me is a big step up from what I normally produce on my albums. It marks so many new achievements, a new level in sound and a seemingly endless amount of musical talent in the shape of the Orchestra, the singers and the instrumentalists.
The whole experience of working with some of the finest musicians and equipment in the world has left me wanting me to do this all over again... But one step at a time.
I hope you all enjoy listening to this album, thank you again to the musicians, artists, photographers and directors, the staff at Abbey Road and most of all.. You, my listeners and supporters.
And especially thank you to the Furry Fandom, for such fantastic support and love, as well as proof.. That together we can all make dreams come true and achieve amazing things."
  -Fox

"I searched for the pieces in the puzzle of my existence in books, in coffee shops, in the quiet of sunrise and loud throngs of people, and found many pieces that almost, almost fit. I sought answers in the forest, but its stillness and beauty made a perfect world, complete. I thought there would be no room in its majesty for me... and then I found you, with your wild eyes and your sleek autumn fur. My steps led me onward without fail into a world like none l had ever seen or imagined. In you I found not the piece I had been missing, but the picture that l was meant to fit into."
  -Kyell Gold
