Game Sound Legend

aka NAMCO Trilogy Box II

This is technically a reprint of 28XA-170, but the track medleys have been split into individual pieces.

01 ~ 11 from: The Return of Ishtar
12 ~ 18 from: Thunder Ceptor
19 ~ 24 from: Hopping Mappy
25 ~ 31 from: Rolling Thunder
32 ~ 48 from: Genji and the Heike Clans
