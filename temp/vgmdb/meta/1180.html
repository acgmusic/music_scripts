Notes

Tracks 15-20 are the "-EXTENDED TRACK-" section. An insert lists the last three bonus backing tracks.

Guitarist Paul Gilbert helped to produce the album and performs on tracks 15-17. First-run bonus was a guitar pick autographed by Gilbert.
