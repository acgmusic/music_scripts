Despite the "Integral" name, not every track from the games is included. Most notably DDS2's 'Rare Devil' battle theme, which was instead downloadable for a while in the publisher's site: http://www.5pb.jp/records/works/exive/dds/dds.html (no longer working). The track was offered as MP3 and WAV, credited to Shoji Meguro, and simply named "Digital Devil Saga".

Discs 1 & 2 cover DIGITAL DEVIL SAGA Avatar Tuner.
Important track name differences from DIGITAL DEVIL SAGA Avatar Tuner Soundtrack CD:
1.20 "Point136" = DDS1 13 "Sand Castle"
2.11 "Never Ending Rain" = DDS1 23 "Configuration"
2.12 "Level Up" = DDS1 25 "Karma"
2.19 "Hunting - Surprise Raid" = DDS1 26 "Hunting"
2.36 "The Rain Stopped" = DDS1 33 "End Theme"
Also, DIGITAL DEVIL SAGA Avatar Tuner Soundtrack CD has an extended version of "Pray" not found on the Integral album.
