Notes

Compilation album commemorating the 35th anniversary of the leading composer Hiroaki Serizawa specializing in popular anime songs in the 80s.
Double-disc set with 35 tracks in total.
Includes a bonus track added on Disc 2, Hiroaki Serizawa's debut single in US which was released on May 24, 2018.
