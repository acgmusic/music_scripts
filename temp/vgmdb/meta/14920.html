Notes: The furigana for the kanji of some of the song titles alters somewhat their meaning.

"FEL FEARY WEL" means "thank you" in ARIA language, while "FEL ARY LU EN TINDHARIA" means "I sing at the end of the world" in said language. The ARIA language is the constructed language used in this album, and in its predecessor: Tindharia no Tane.
