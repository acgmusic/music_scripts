Notes

5-discs Box set containing two movie soundtrack released in the LP era and three original album released in the 1990s, all reprinted and recorded with the latest digital remastered sound source in HQCD. It comes with a clear type plastic box, a commentary booklet and the original reproduction of LP jacket, obi, lyrics to CD size for movie soundtrack. Original 1990s albums come with their original obi band.
