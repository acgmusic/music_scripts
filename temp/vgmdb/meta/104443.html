Notes

Tracks 1~4 contain an original symphonic suite inspired by the Japanese science fiction book series Ginga Kojiki Gundan (銀河乞食軍団), written by Masahiro Noda.
Composition, Arrangement & Conducting: Kohei Tanaka
Performance: 星涯星系軍軍楽隊

Track 5 is a drama.
Planning: Hiroshi Yamaguchi
Script: 大出秀明
