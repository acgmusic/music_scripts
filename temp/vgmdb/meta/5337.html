According to Yoshitaka Hirota's official website (http://www.twintail-studio.com/), he worked on this CD.

In an interview with Square Enix Music Online (http://www.squareenixmusic.com/interviews/hirota.html), Hirota states that he worked on the main theme "Nemuru Mizuumi" (Sleeping Lake) and "Naada Brahma" (This World is Made of Sounds) i.e. Track 5 and Track 8.

Track 1 "Hitotsu no Inori" is also on Noriko Mitose's album savon where the composer and arranger is credited as Naruki Endo.

Album contains five vocal tracks (1, 5, 6, 11, 13) and nine instrumentals (2~4, 7~10, 12, 14).
