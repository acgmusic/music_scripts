Notes

Soundtrack to the games "Sou, Atashitachi wa Konna ni mo Rifujin na Sekai ni Ikiteiru no dara yo" and "Sou, Atashitachi wa Konna ni mo Rifujin na Sekai ni Ikiteiru no dara yo 3 * Kono Sekai de 2 no Hatsubai Yotei wa Arimasen." contained as both WAV and Ogg Vorbis files on a DVD-ROM.

M1~29 (FM Arrange Version)
M30~58 (Windows Arrange Version)
M59~64 (Unused Tracks)

*4-5 and 6-9 on the FM Version have the same audio content.

M13 and M42 are instrumental arrangements of "Auld Lang Syne."
