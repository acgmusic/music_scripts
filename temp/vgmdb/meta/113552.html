Notes

Virtual Video Game Orchestra is proud to present our eleventh project, "Prologue (Book One)", from Golden Sun in celebration of its 20th anniversary. Along with "End Card: Page One" played during the credits, this project features more than 450 recording submissions from musicians across the Golden Sun community and the global video game music community at-large.
