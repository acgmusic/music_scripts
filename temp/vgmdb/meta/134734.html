Notes

A collection of songs that were all released on the anniversary of the 2011 Touhoku earthquake and tsunami, each in a different year, including two album originals.
Proceeds from sales of the album were donated to the governments of affected prefectures and the Japan Red Cross for the purpose of disaster recovery.

M-09 - NHK Touhoku Earthquake and Tsunami 10th-Anniversary Drama Special "Anata no Soba de Ashita ga Warau" Theme Song
