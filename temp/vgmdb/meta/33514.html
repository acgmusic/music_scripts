Notes

As a present for those who purchased both Sorcerian Original Soundtrack Vol.1 & 2.

Features MP3 recordings of all five games (Sorcerian, Sorcerian UTILITY Vol.1, Sorcerian System Scenario Vol.1, Sengoku Sorcerian, and Pyramid Sorcerian).

The MP3s are recorded from the following platforms:
- PC-8801 (FM)
- PC-9801 (FM and BEEP)
- X1turbo (FM and PSG)

As with Vol. 1 however, the 5 songs from the "Romancia" scenario are not included.

Additionally, the filenames for tracks 181 and 285 from Sengoku Sorcerian (PC98 BEEP and FM versions) are mislabeled. Specifically, instead of 「異形の地」 (Strange Land), it should be 「異教の地」 (Pagan Land) instead.
