Notes

This is a compilation album containing music posted on or significant to the Japanese video site Nico Nico Douga.

01 Dancing☆Samurai: original Nico song
    Composition/Arrangement/Lyrics: mathru (Kanimiso-P)
    Vocals: Gakupo Kamui

02 Balalaika (original version): second OP theme from Kirarin Revolution
    Composition/Arrangement: Shigeki Sako
    Vocals: Kirari Tsukishima (CV: Koharu Kusumi)
    Lyrics: BULGE

03 Hikyou Sentai Urotander: original Nico song
    Composition/Arrangement/Lyrics: Shin P (シンP)
    Vocals: KAITO, MEIKO, Miku Hatsune

04 Mi-w-Na-w-Gi-w-T-w-Te-w-Ki-w-Ta-www (Shinobue Kindanshoujou L5): original Nico piece
    Composition/Performance: Shimashima P (しましまP)

05 Ora Tokyo sa Iguda: original Ikuzo Yoshi song from 1984
    Composition/Vocals/Lyrics: Ikuzo Yoshi
    Arrangement: Yutaka Nomura

06 Yumemiru Dolly (Original Title: DOLLY SONG): remix of the scat section of Loituma's version of Ievan Polkka, created by a group of Italian producers
    Composition: traditional
    Arrangement: Luca Ontino, Simone Bocchino, Valentia Dante
    Performance: HOLLY DOLLY (ホリー・ドリー)
    Lyrics: Barbara Tanzini, Luca Ontino, Simone Bocchino, Valentia Dante, Sari Kauranen, Anita Lehtola-Tollin, Timo Väänänen, Hanni-Mari Autere

07 Hato: original Nico song
    Composition/Arrangement/Lyrics: Len Kazami (風見レン)
    Vocals: Miku Hatsune

08 Hajimete no Tomodachi ~ the answer: original Nico song
    Composition/Arrangement/Vocals/Lyrics: Hyadain

09 Cheetah Girl: arrangement of the in-game music from Cheetahmen II
    Composition: unknown
    Arrangement: KTG (Aliced Twilightz)
    Vocals/Lyrics: Linco (Aliced Twilightz)

10 Black★Rock Shooter: original Nico song
    Composition/Arrangement/Lyrics: ryo
    Vocals: Miku Hatsune

11 Cirno no Perfect Sansuu Kyoushitsu: arrangement of "Beloved Tomboyish Girl" from Touhou Koumakyou (original album: Touhou Hyousetsu Kashuu [IO-0147])
    Composition: ZUN (Team Shanghai Alice)
    Arrangement: ARM
    Vocals: miko (Alternative ending)
    Voice: quim
    Lyrics: Yoshimi Youno

12 Nico Nico Douga Ryuusei Musume: medley
    Arrangement: Shimo (しも)
