Notes

The first official release of the score by Robert J. Walsh and Johnny Douglas. Includes the opening theme and more than 45 mins. of unreleased music from seasons 1-3.

Available in Bumblebee, Optimus Prime and Megatron variants. Also available in Starscream, Soundwave & Autobot/Decepticon split variants.
