Notes

"A 4+ hour psychedelic “guest” mix, concocted by Nmesh, full of retro video game / PC music, chock full of old game soundtracks, commercial bits, SFX, PSA’s, and completely warped edits. Tiny Mix Tapes has been kidnapped by ninjas, and they need YOU to prove you’re a bad enough dude to save them. Venture through 10 different themed worlds, and rescue TMT staff members along the way, but be careful … there are plenty of classic video game death scenarios that could potentially disrupt your quest. Grab a couple heart containers, and sally forth on the greatest pixelized audio-adventure yet!"

Limited Edition 3 Cassette Box Set.
Pro-dubbed and imprinted. Transparent red shells with white ink housed in a 'VHS Style' case.
The set includes full wrap around cover art, an insert with sample list, a limited edition 'Warp Zone' button, and a hand written thank you note on the back of a 'Warp Zone' note-card.

Compiled, mixed and thrown into a 16-bit vat of acid by Alex Koenig
Premiered by Tiny Mix Tapes
Digital release on November 26, 2014
