Notes

The team behind VVGO have decided to put the power to make music in the hands of our community! Until now, we have offered full-ensemble or choral-only recording projects for anyone to join. With the VVGO Summer Mixtape, we open the door for smaller-scale pieces and a larger volume of pieces to manifest.

Here are those pieces!
