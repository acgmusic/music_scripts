Notes

"After many years, the first chilean VGM compilation is here! Includes song arrangements of Undertale, Metal Slug, Donkey Kong Country, Doom, Metroid, Megaman... And more!.
Includes music by YZYX, Psycho Crusher, ThePlasmas, KBT, Pokérus, Satou and special guests."


Luis Ramirez - Viola on track 12
JC - Additional Synths on track 16
Milton Jara - Sax on track 19
Ítalo "Dark Sness" Donoso - Symphonic Arrangements on track 9

Album Mastered by Manuel Xavier (YZYX) at e6 Sound.
Album Art by Patricio Thielemann (Pokérus).
Tracks produced between 2014 and 2016.
