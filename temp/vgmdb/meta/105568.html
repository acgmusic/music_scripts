About This Competition
At the end of 2007 and beginning of 2008, SquareSound held an arrangement competition celebrating the career of Nobuo Uematsu. We had 53 entrants and the winner (Andrew Aversa) won two VIP tickets to the USA premier of the Distant Worlds: Music from Final Fantasy Concert. The arrangements on this page are all the entries in the competition.
