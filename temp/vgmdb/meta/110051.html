Notes

Yellow-colored vinyl. Initial run limited to 1,000 copies.

Every side of the record has a special name:
1A The Jungle
1B Unfamiliar Territory
2A Monkey Mines
2B The Last Stand


All tracks composed by David Wise except * [1B-09, 2A-04, 2A-05, 2B-06, 2B-07, 2B-08] composed by Evelyne [sic!] Fischer and † [1B-08] composed by Robin Beanland.

[1A-02 also composed by Eveline Fischer despite not being credited on this album.]

These covers were recreated from the original hex data by Jammin' Sam Miller.

Using hex SPC data crudely converted to MIDI, Jammin' Sam Miller painstakingly recreated DKC's soundtrack note by note, by finding the original equipment used to create it, translating the MIDI into a modern studio context, adding in keyboard samples, and re-mixing the sounds with added effects and mastering. To find out more about his process watch an explanatory video here: https://cutt.ly/ulUHE6J or head over to this thread: https://cutt.ly/ElUKklj
