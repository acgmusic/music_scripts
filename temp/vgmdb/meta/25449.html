Notes

A collection of songs from the second season of GUNSLINGER GIRL, titled GUNSLINGER GIRL -IL TEATRINO-.

M-01 - opening theme
    Lyricist/Composer: KOKIA
    Arranger: KOKIAn'S
    Vocals: KOKIA

M-02,03,06~10 - 1st ending theme (episodes 1-12)
    Lyricist/Composer: Jun Maeda
    Arranger: ANANT-GARDE EYES
    Vocals: Aoi Tada (M-02)
                Lia (M-03)
                CV: Kana Akutsu (M-06)

                CV: Risa Mizuno (M-09)

    Lyricist/Composer: English folk song
    Arranger: Tatsuhiko Fuyuno
    Vocals: Aoi Tada
    Programming: Tatsuhiko Fuyuno (orizzonte)

    Keyboards: Tomoko Kato (orizzonte)

M-05 - 2nd ending theme (episode 13)
    Lyricist/Composer: Jun Maeda
    Arranger: ANANT-GARDE EYES
