The sound programs at Toshiba-IHI Pavilion consist of those for "Waiting Space" on the first floor, "Global Vision Theater in the air and "Park of Light and Water".
Side I of this compact record contains the extracted sound effects of "12-channel Multiplex Sound System" of Global Vision.
On Side II you can hear "Folksong Medley of the World" played at the waiting space and also the essence of the three-dimensional sound effects at the underground park of light and water.

(1) Title: Toshiba-IHI Pavilion, "Global Vision"
(2) Theme: "Hope" -Light and Man-
