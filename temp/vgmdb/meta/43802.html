Notes

At Peace is a new and innovative producer whose style and sound bring something completely new to the Hardcore table. His music spans across Hardcore, Trancecore and Freeform, but with a trademark hard kick.

The Holy Gates is At Peace and Tears Of Kunlun's debut album project, and showcases At Peace’s unique sound. The album contains tracks such as Cost Of Sacrifice, which grabbed the attention of followers in the Freeform scene. It also features The Price I’m About To Pay, which achieved BBC Radio air time in January this year.

The album’s music and artwork are closely linked, and the whole project forms something of a spiritual and emotional journey. This release is a single audio CD, with each CD face printed uniquely containing a word or name that inspired the album. The release contains 14 tracks; 12 full length mastered songs, a prelude and an interlude.
