Notes

Complete CD reproduction from original master tape (produced in 1975) discovered in 2009!!!

Liner Notes: Matsuo Ono
Original board commentary full text reprint: Osamu Tezuka, Hideki Yoshida, Matsuo Ono
若林恵, 佐々木暁

Notes from Parent Album:
This work is an album that collects the sound effects of TV animation [Astro Boy], which was produced weekly for four years from 1963 to 1966.

■Production Staff+Recording Data
Sound Organizer & Sound Composition: Matsuo Ono
Producer: Hideki Yoshida
Planning: Acryl Tower Process
              Osamu Kuramoto + Kishio Oyama
Cover Design: Kazuhiko Suyama
Thanks to: Osamu Tezuka & Tezuka Production Co.
Recorded Date: Sept 6,7,1975
Location: Sogosha Co./Recording Studio
ATOM'S RECORDING GROUP

(additional from insert)
Co-planning: Minoru Yoshida/吉田美能留
Acoustic Director: Takehisa Kosugi/小杉武久, Iwao Takahashi/高橋 巖, 桜井勝美
Recording Mixer: Tetsuya Ohashi/大橋鉄矢, Mitsuru Kashiwabara
Scripter: 伊藤久枝


*the official CD reprint "VZCG-712" which comes from the same original master tape of 1975 LP release, divided into 13 tracks according to the intention of Mr. Matsuo Ono and the classification of the original LP (Kojima recording in 1975, and Victor reissue in 1979), and the individual sound effects are indexed.
Original 1975 LP unlisted credits from "Tetsuwan Atom" theme song at the beginning [Lyrics: Shuntaro Tanikawa / Composition and Arrangement: Tatsuo Takai] and instrumental theme song as last track later listed in Victor reissue in 1979.
