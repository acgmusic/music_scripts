Notes

Isao Sasaki's all-time best album commemorating his 45th anniversary since his debut.
It features an original illustrated cover by Leiji Matsumoto, and the booklet includes comments and explanations by Isao Sasaki.
25 songs in total, all digitally remastered.

Vocals by Isao Sasaki, except Track-06 with the artist mentioned below.
