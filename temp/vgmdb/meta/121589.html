Notes

Songs from various anime series, arranged into NES/Famicom chiptunes using Famitracker.

What started as an idea to make some upbeat chiptunes during quarantine turned into monthly releases. From April to December of 2020,

I made a cover per month and released them as singles with custom track art for each song. Throughout 2021 I covered 10 more tracks

and released the full album of energetic beepy-boopy goodness you see here.
