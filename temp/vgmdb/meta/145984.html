Notes

For our eleventh mixtape, we challenged our members to come up with arrangements of spooky video game music tunes. Our members responded by coming up with a number of different inventive arrangements. We hope you enjoy VVGhosts!

The team behind VVGO has decided to put the power to make music in the hands of our community! We open the door for smaller-scale pieces and a larger volume of pieces to manifest.
