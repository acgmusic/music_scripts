Notes

European version of the promotional pre-order album, with materials printed in English.

Notes from Parent Album:
Preorder bonus for Chrono Trigger DS in Japan.

Square awarded 3,000 special editions with black packaging and discs to members of its Japanese fan club in February 2009.

The medley consists of: Premonition, Guardia Millenial Fair, Longing of the Wind (Wind Scene), Kaeru's Theme (Frog's Theme), Decisive Battle with Maou (Battle with Magus), Epilogue ~To Beloved Friends~ (Epilogue ~To Good Friends~) and Outskirts of Time (To Far Away Times).
