Notes

Last up in my end-of-2021 trend of releasing collections of my older works, this here is a batch of my game remixes that aren’t currently represented in one of my other albums. Nothing here is duplicated on Game Remix, Tracker Homage, or part of either American Pixels or The American Album. True, several tracks here were created for an OverClocked Remix album or some other collective, but I thought it might be nice if I were to assemble the tracks together as a single (and fairly massive) Mazedude set. And thus I present Game Remix, Assortment.

Fair warning: if you’ve been following my work for some time, you probably already have these tracks. This collection is not remastered, and there are no new “surprise” remixes on here that haven’t already been released elsewhere. It’s simply an assemblage of my works that all fall into the same category, and this isn’t even all of them. So there’s really no reason for you to pay for this collection if you already have these remixes via OverClocked, my website, or elsewhere. Just saying.
