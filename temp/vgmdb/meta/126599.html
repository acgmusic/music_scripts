Notes

Special album celebration for 20th anniversary (April 2009) of Radio Kansai's "Seishun Radimenia", a pioneer of Japanese anime song radio program featuring Kazuo Iwasaki and Kaori Minami! The album includes 20 representative songs of "Seishun Radimenia" selected by request from among many anime songs beyond the boundaries of manufacturers.
