Notes

Promotional soundtrack album exclusively sold at Cave Matsuri, containing the soundtracks to four Cave games.

Puzzle! Mushihimetama: 1.01-20
Music Composer: Masashi Yano (SuperSweep), MASA-KING, Kizakura, Natsuko Naitou, Naohiro Saito [1-08 (stage 4) -from the composer's official site], 椎葉

Track 17
Words: WISSU
Music: Natsuko Naitou
Guitar: 中西祐理
Vocal: Reco (CV: Yui Yoshii)

Track 18
Words & Music: Natsuko Naitou
Vocal: Reco & The Copper Shoes Choir (銅の靴合唱団) (Yui Yoshii, Maomao, Maria, Rieko, Asakichi)

Himitsu no Hanapoko: 1.21-41
Music Composer: Ryuichi Yabuki

Dangun FEVERON: 2.01-17
Music Composer: Nanpei Misawa
Sound Effects: Ryuichi Yabuki*
* Yabuki is credited as a composer as well, but he says in the booklet of DANGUN FEVERON [SGCD-0004] that he only did sound effects.

DonPachi: 2.18-33
Music Composer: Ryuichi Yabuki

Mastering Engineer: Ryoji Uezu
