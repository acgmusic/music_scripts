Notes

The original soundtrack for "Ai no Kiseki: Doctor Norman Monogatari," an animated TV special that aired on TV Asahi on December 24, 1982.

Music Composed & Arranged by Chikara Ueda

02. opening theme (Lyrics: You Yamamoto)
      Lyricist: Suzi Kim
      Vocals: Suzi

      [Translation: 藤原真理子]

Produced, Arranged, and All songs composed by Chikara Ueda
Performed by C. Ueda and the Power Station

Recording Director: Kotaro Nakamura
Recording and Mixing Engineer: Masaaki Saito
Assistant Engineer: Takao Suga
Recorded at King Record 1st and 2nd Studio
   from October 16th to November 18th, 1982, Tokyo Japan
Album Design: W. Sakaguchi
