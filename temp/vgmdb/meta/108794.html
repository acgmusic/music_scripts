Notes

M01~17: Departure Melodies
M18~39: Sound Effects, etc.
M40~45: Platform Announcements (Announcer: 豊田真由美)
M46~56: In-Train Announcements (Announcer: 石毛美奈子)

M11~12 are arrangements of the opening of TV Anime 'Mobile Suit Gundam', originally composed by Takeo Watanabe.
M13~14 are arrangements of the cheering song of baseball team 'Saitama Seibu Lions', originally composed by 成田洋明.
M15~16 are arrangements of the opening of TV Anime 'Galaxy Express 999', originally composed by Masaaki Hirao.
M17 is an arrangement of the opening of NHK Drama 'Tsubasa', originally composed by Angela Aki.

M01~2, 11~12, 17, 32, 42~43, 50~54, and 56 are from the Seibu Shinjuku Line.
M03~4, 13~16, 36, 40~41, 44~49, and 54~55 are from the Seibu Ikebukuro Line.
M05 is from the Seibu Haijime Line.
M07~8 are from the Seibu Tamako Line.
M09~10, 36, and 46 are from the Seibu Chichibu Line.
M14 is from the Seibu Sayama Line.
