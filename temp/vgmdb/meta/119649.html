Notes

Pre-order Bonus for "Nayuta no Kiseki: Ad Astra." Music is the same as the original PSP release.

Notes from Parent Album:
A complete version of the soundtrack is included with the NIS America release of The Legend of Nayuta - Boundless Trails.  All deluxe editions include the Digital Soundtrack, and limited editions include a CD version titled Starfall Symphonies.

All Music by Falcom Sound Team jdk (Hayato Sonoda, Takahiro Unisuga, Saki Momiyama), with outsourced work by Noriyuki Kamikura/Yukihiro Jindo and uncredited music from Ryo Takeshita/Masanori Osaki.

Composer breakdown leaked by an anonymous user with ties to Falcom: https://www65.atwiki.jp/falcom_staff/pages/142.html
In addition to this, the bgmtbl.tbl file within the game has composer codes in it, which are mostly consistent with the leaked credits: https://www65.atwiki.jp/falcom_staff/pages/109.html
