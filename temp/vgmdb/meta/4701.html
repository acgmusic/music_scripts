Notes

Disc 1, track 13 has "の間" missing on the back cover.

Disc 1, Track 19 on this reprint is incorrectly spelled as "Bloody Teas" rather than the correct "Bloody Tears" (as it was on the original print). Moreover, all instances of "塔" were changed to "台" (both are valid kanji).

Disc 1, Track 23 changed all instances of "間" to "部屋".

Disc 2, Track 11 corrected the spelling from "Dracura" to "Dracula".

Disc 2, Tracks 15 and 18 have "#1" missing on the back cover.

Disc 2, Tracks 18 & 31 on this reprint incorrectly spell the word "Enemy" as "Enermy". It was correct on the original print.

Disc 2, Track 22 is incorrectly spelled as "Ripe Seedes" on the back of the album. It is spelled correctly as "Ripe Seeds" in the booklet, as well as both locations on the original album press run.

Disc 2, Track 35 changed all instances of "of" to "Of".

Other than some minor punctuation changes, the rest of the track list remains the same, meaning that the other misspelled titles "Battle of the Holly", "Phyco Warror" and "Passpied" persisted.

Notes from Parent Album:
Disc 1: Super Castlevania IV

Disc 2: Castlevania: The Adventure and Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge

   01~12: Castlevania: The Adventure
   13~35: Castlevania II: Belmont's Revenge

Disc 2 Track 27 is an arrangement of Passepied from Suite Bergamasque by Claude Debussy.
Disc 2 Track 30 is an arrangement of Chromatic Fantasy and Fugue (BWV 903) by Johann Sebastian Bach.
