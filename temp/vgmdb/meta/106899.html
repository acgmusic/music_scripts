Notes

This 2LP soundtrack, featuring original art by mushbuh, contains 42 tracks from Kemmei Adachi, Nobuyuki Shimizu, and Kouji Niikura's soundtrack to LocoRoco, remastered for and pressed on vibrant yellow and pink vinyl.
