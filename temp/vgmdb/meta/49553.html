Notes

A tribute album celebrating the 15th anniversary of Hikaru Utada, contains covers by various artists.

M-08 and M-13 are songs from Kingdom Hearts I and II respectively.

Performers:
M-01 - Yousui Inoue
M-02 - Ringo Sheena
M-03 - Yasuyuki Okamura
M-04 - Ayumi Hamasaki
M-05 - hanaregumi (Takashi Nagazumi)
M-06 - AI
M-07 - Kazuya Yoshii
M-08 - LOVE PSYCHEDELICO
M-09 - Miliyah Kato
M-10 - ohashiTrio
M-11 - tofubeats with BONNIE PINK
M-12 - KIRINJI
M-13 - Jimmy Jam & Terry Lewis feat. Peabo Bryson

The album is priced 2100 Yen in iTunes Store.
