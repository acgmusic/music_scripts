Notes

This tribute album to the shonen genre includes new recordings of BGM from anime "Saint Seiya" and reorchestrations of BGM from animes "Dragon Ball (Z)" and "Space Cobra".
This double CD also includes covers of opening themes from famous shonen TV series.
The second CD focuses on Saint Seiya. The director's cut versions are versions containing previously unreleased passages that composer Seiji Yokoyama had crossed out on his handwritten scores and which were never recorded.
