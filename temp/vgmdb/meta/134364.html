Notes

Team up with Tournament Arc for the biggest video game album of the year!

TOURNAMENT ARC is the self-titled debut studio album of the Boston-based troupe, and the culmination of countless hours of creative energies over the past 18 months.

This 50-minute sonic experience will inspire a return to nostalgic roots, with 12 spectacular tracks from–and inspired by–video games, spanning decades of fan-favorite melodies from games like FINAL FANTASY VII, CHRONO TRIGGER, Persona 5, Journey, Bastion, The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, NieR: Automata, and more.

"Thank you so much for purchasing Tournament Arc’s debut album! It really means the world to all of us. A lot of hard work went into it and, from the bottom of my heart, I hope you enjoy it." - Mason Lieberman, Music Director
