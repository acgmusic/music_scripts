Notes

LEVEL 2-1

A musical tribute to the character of Shadow the Hedgehog, from the Sonic the Hedgehog video games.


CREDITS

Gnegon Galek - arrangement, bass guitar (tracks 2 & 6), electric guitar (tracks 1, 2 & 7), vocals
Flyghtning - programming, mixing & mastering
Kelen - electric guitar (tracks 5, 6 & 8)


M1 - "False Start"
Written by Gnegon Galek
Contains a musical motif from "Vengeance Is Mine" performed by Jun Senoue, from "Multi-Dimensional: Sonic Adventure 2 Original Sound Track", Marvelous Entertainment, 2001
Contains a sample from "Universal Soldier", TriStar Pictures, 1992

M2 - "I Am... All of Me"
Written & originally performed by Crush 40
From "Lost and Found: Shadow the Hedgehog Vocal Trax", Wave Master Entertainment, 2006
Contains a sample from "When Will They Shoot?" performed by Ice Cube, from "The Predator", Priority Records, 1992
Contains musical motives from "System: Battle Menu" & "System: Main Menu" composed by Jun Senoue, from "Shadow the Hedgehog Original Soundtrax", Wave Master, 2006

M3 - "Rhythm and Balance"
Written & composed by Fumie Kumatani
Originally arranged by Heigo Tani
Lyrics translated by Shinobu Shindo
Originally performed by Everette Bradly
From "Multi-Dimensional: Sonic Adventure 2 Original Sound Track", Marvelous Entertainment, 2001
Contains a sample from "Throw It All Away" performed by Everette Bradly, from "Cuts Unleashed: SA2 Vocal Collection", Marvelous Entertainment, 2001

M4 - "The Supernatural"
Written & composed by Fumie Kumatani
Originally arranged by Heigo Tani
Lyrics translated by Shinobu Shindo
Originally performed by Everette Bradly
From "Multi-Dimensional: Sonic Adventure 2 Original Sound Track", Marvelous Entertainment, 2001
Additional arrangement by Armadillomon
Contains a sample from "Doom 3", Activision, 2004
Contains a musical motif from "Event: 3 Black Noises (Revival... Chaos Control... Reflection)" composed by Kenichi Tokoi, also from the aforementioned album

M5 - "Battle Highway"
Composed by Kenichi Tokoi
Additional arrangement by Kelen
Additional vocals by Flyghtning
Original version not released on an album
Contains a sample from "JoJo's Bizarre Adventure", A.P.P.P., 1993
Contains a sample from "Reservoir Dogs", Miramax Films, 1992
Contains a sample from "Sonic Battle", Sega / THQ, 2003

M6 - "Lava Shelter"
Composed by Jun Senoue
From "Shadow the Hedgehog Original Soundtrax", Wave Master, 2006
Lyrics written by Gnegon Galek
Lyric translation to Vehturian by Flyghtning
Floppy drive programming & recording by Speaktrap
Contains a sample from "Shadow the Hedgehog", Sega, 2005
Contains a sample from "Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell: Pandora Tomorrow", Ubisoft, 2004
Contains a sample from "Faith" performed by Limp Bizkit, from "Three Dollar Bill, Y'all$", Flip / Interscope, 1997

M7 - "You're a Woman, I'm a Machine"
Written & originally performed by Death from Above 1979
From "You're a Woman, I'm a Machine", Last Gang, 2004
Contains a sample of Navy Seal Copypasta reading by The Volgun, published on YouTube in 2013
Contains a sample from "Ghetto Red Hot (Hip-Hop Mix)" performed by Super Cat, from "Ghetto Red Hot", Columbia, 1992
Contains a sample of "Breakfast of Champions" audiobook, written by Kurt Vonnegut, narrated by John Malkovich, Audible Studios, 2015

M8 - "Reflection"
Written by Gnegon Galek
Additional arrangement by Flyghtning
Bass guitar by Speaktrap
Contains a sample from "Sligus Sligus: Satan Prychadzij!", KJM, 2009



Packaging artwork by Richard Vollebregt, assembled by Gnegon Galek

Songs arranged using Renoise and FL Studio 11 & 12.5 software
Songs performed using Shure C607N, Korg padControl, GMR custom bass, Roter custom 5-string bass, Fender Stratocaster modified by Krzysztof Stokłosa, Sterling JP 50, Line 6 POD UX1, Line 6 POD UX

Special thanks in completely random order to Ola Wojtewicz, Nukot, Kamchatkabyrd, Kasumisty, GameMusic.pl, Gracze Słuchacze, the Sonic Fans PL group, dr Xymena Synak-Pskit, Agnieszka Prusicka, Anna Ellwart, Yati, SuzakuGuardian, Primpy, FabulousAhoy, Luna Pubben, Keik, Ixi, Armadillomon, Zalibarek, LittleElvis
