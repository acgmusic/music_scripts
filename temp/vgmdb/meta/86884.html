Notes

A collection of orchestral works by Taro Iwashiro, composed for various films and NHK TV productions.

     M-01 - from NHK's 50th Anniversary Commemoration Drama "A River, Flowing back to the Ocean..." (Kawa, Itsuka Umi e)
     M-02 - from the movie "The Samurai I Loved" (Semishigure)
     M-03 - from the NHK Taiga Drama "Aoi Tokugawa Sandai"
     M-04 - from the movie "Spring Snow" (Haru no Yuki)
     M-05 - from the movie "Blood and Bones" (Chi to Hone)
M-06,09 - from the NHK Taiga Drama "Yoshitsune"
     M-07 - from the movie "SHINOBI"
     M-08 - from the NHK Drama "The Chatham School Affair" (Hiiro no Kioku)
     M-10 - from the original album "12 Vox"
