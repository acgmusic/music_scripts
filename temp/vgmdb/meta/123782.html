Notes

Virtual Video Game Orchestra is proud to present our fifteenth project, "Staff Roll" from The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword, along with credits arrangement "Fi's Gratitude", in collaboration with Zelda Universe! Together, these two performances feature 352 submissions on 84 unique instruments from 133 individual players across the global video game music community.

"STAFF ROLL"
from The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword (2011)
Music by Hajime Wakai (若井淑), Shiho Fujii (藤井志帆), Mahito Yokota (横田真人), Takeshi Hama (はまたけし), and Koji Kondo (近藤浩治)
Arranged by Finny Jacob Zeleny, Brandon Harnish, Thomas Håkanson, and John MacKay

"FI'S GRATITUDE"
from The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword (2011)
Composed by Takeshi Hama (はまたけし)
Arranged by Finny Jacob Zeleny
