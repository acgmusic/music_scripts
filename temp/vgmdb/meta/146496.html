Notes

Takeshi Kusao's 1st Original Album.
It contains 10 songs and one instrumental track. Among the songs, 5 are arrangements from the anime "Yoroiden Samurai Troopers" (aka "Ronin Warriors") and 5 are new songs.


Lyricist: Natsumi Watanabe (01, 03~10)
Arranger: Osamu Totsuka, Toru Okada (01)
               Toru Okada (03~10)


01. Arrangement of the image song "Honoo no Soldier" from TV anime "Ronin Warriors"
Composer: Osamu Totsuka

02. Instrumental
Composer: Toru Okada

03. Cover of the 1st Opening Theme from TV anime "Ronin Warriors"
Composer: Yasuhiko Shigemura

04. Cover of the 2nd Opening Theme from TV anime "Ronin Warriors"
Composer: Yasuhiko Shigemura

05. Arrangement of the image song "Wonder -Toki no Nageru Gimonfu-" from TV Anime "Ronin Warriors"
Composer: Yusuke Nakamura

06. Original Song
Composer: Yusuke Nakamura

07. Original Song
Composer: Katsumi Yamaura

08. Cover of the 1st Ending Theme from TV anime "Ronin Warriors"
Composer: Midori Karashima

09. Original Song
Composer: Toru Okada

10. Original Song
Composer: Toru Okada
