Notes

The original physical and digital versions of this album included defective versions of three songs which were missing parts: 2.11, 2.18, and 2.24. Replacement discs with the catalog number LC2556<TGCS-12856>-2 were sent and included with later sales of the album, and the digital version of the album was later updated in May.
