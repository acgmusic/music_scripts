Notes

"BE-MUSIC Special Selection 2021 is now ready! There are once again over 120 tracks, so it's taken some time, but we hope people are happy to hear BMS tracks they like, or to discover more music from BMS events!

This time, music from the following events is included:
- 第三回BMS衆議院選
- PABAT 2021 Seasons -Bakery tour-
- BMSパッケージ企画「ゆるゆる」
- "Nancyatte" A-1 ClimaX -Just kidding-
- Bit Memory Squarewaves
- beatissimo
- Be Music in the Dark

As with previous years, a bonus disc with a few remixes and extended versions is included as well.

As always, we've done our best to include a wide variety of representative songs from each event. Thank you to everyone who participated with the inclusion of their songs!
This time, we've additionally prepared a sabun package with some new patterns for some of the songs included in the compilation. Since BE-MUSIC Special Selection is a project that exists because of BMS, the intention this time was to find a way to emphasize the gameplay side of BMS too, and give players who may have already played through the events some additional new content to enjoy. Thank you to all the charters who helped with this new endeavor by taking time out of their schedule to create new patterns for the package!"
