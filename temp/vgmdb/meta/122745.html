Notes

Start your engines! It’s time to launch VVGO's third community-created album, this time celebrating 30 years of Mario Kart and 25 years of Diddy Kong Racing! Featuring 11 tracks from games across all platforms and genres, VVGO proudly showcases talent on all kinds of instruments from orchestral to traditional.
