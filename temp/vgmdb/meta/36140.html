Notes

Limited reissued album (5000 pieces) as disc number "91" of Columbia "ANIMEX 1200" series.

Notes from Parent Album:
Composition: Seiji Yokoyama
Performer: Andromeda Harmonic Orchestra
Chorus: Columbia Male Choir (1,6,7,10,11)
Scat: Kazuko Kawashima (7,8,11,15*)

Illustration: Shingo Araki, Michi Himeno

This album contains music from the second movie (Saint Seiya: Kamigami no Atsuki Tatakai). Some of them (02,07,10) have been reprised also in the second Asgard TV series.
12~15 are unused bonus tracks: those tracks contain music from the first movie (Saint Seiya: Jashin Eris) and some of them (beginning of 12,14,15) have been reprised in first TV series. The beginning of 13 contains the Shun's theme reprised on first TV serie, the main Orpheus Lyra's theme from first movie and a little part of Mime melody from the second Asgard TV series.

*uncredited
