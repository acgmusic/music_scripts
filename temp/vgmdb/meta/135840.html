Notes

For our eighth mixtape, we asked our members to arrange their favorite town themes. Our members responded by coming up with a number of different inventive town theme arrangements. We hope you'll join us in exploring all the places you'll VVGO!

The team behind VVGO has decided to put the power to make music in the hands of our community! We open the door for smaller-scale pieces and a larger volume of pieces to manifest.
