Notes

"The Sounds of Picnic Province is a seamless album that captures the ambiences & low-key mixes of the background music between all the major areas of Chicory: A Colorful Tale.

It's a bit of an experimental take on a soundtrack album, meant as a one-shot listening experience rather than individual tracks. For the best experience, download the FLACs & load them up in a gapless audio player with big comfy headphones and your favorite beverage
