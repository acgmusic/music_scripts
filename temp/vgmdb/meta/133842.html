Notes

On our seventh mixtape, we opened the floodgates to our members to arrange any vgm piece. Our members responded by coming up with a number of different inventive arrangements. We hope you enjoy the first volume of our Now That's What I Call VVGO mixtape series!

The team behind VVGO has decided to put the power to make music in the hands of our community! We open the door for smaller-scale pieces and a larger volume of pieces to manifest.

Album Art: Lilac Flute
2D Artist: Jordy de Lat
Tech Art: Jordy de Lat
Track Mastering: Finny Jacob Zeleny
