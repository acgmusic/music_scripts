Notes

All Music by Falcom Sound Team jdk (Hayato Sonoda, Wataru Ishibashi, Takahide Murayama), with outsourced work by Yukihiro Jindo/Kohei Wada and uncredited music from Maiko Hattori.

Composer breakdown leaked by an anonymous user with ties to Falcom: https://www65.atwiki.jp/falcom_staff/pages/142.html

Composition:
Hayato Sonoda (Disc 1: 1, 2, 4, 7, 9, 10, 13, 14, 15, 20, 24~31, 32 33 / Disc 2: 1, 2, 7, 12, 13, 17, 19, 20, 21, 23, 25, 26)
Wataru Ishibashi (Disc 1: 1, 3, 5, 6, 8, 11, 12, 18, 19, 22, 23 / Disc 2: 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 10, 11, 14, 15, 16, 24)
Takahide Murayama (Disc 1: 21 / Disc 2: 18)
Maiko Hattori (uncredited) (Disc 2: 22)
Yukihiro Jindo (contractor) (Disc 1: 16, 17 / Disc 2: 9)

Arrangement:
Yukihiro Jindo (contractor) (Disc 1: 1 / Disc 2: 15)
Kohei Wada (contractor) (Disc 2: 23, 25, 26)

Notes:
• D1-1 was composed collaboratively by Sonoda and Ishibashi, and arranged by Jindo.
• D1-24~31 are event BGM that play during the Suite "Madrigal of the White Magnolia" (組曲 白き花のマドリガル)
• D1-16~17 and D2-9 confirmed by Jindo himself on his blog. He said he wrote this piece in the same mood as Rimsky-Korsakov's "Scheherazade": https://blog.goo.ne.jp/yukihiro_jindo/e/2f4454fc9167a5b9e0c6e7e7c64b6aeb
