Notes

"Wintry Mix" is VVGO’s second community-run mixtape, and is the sequel to the successful launch of the Summer Mixtape consisting of winter-themed tracks. Featuring 85 performers from our community from across the globe, VVGO proudly showcases talent on all kinds on instruments from orchestral to traditional.

Album art by Sophia W (herb).
