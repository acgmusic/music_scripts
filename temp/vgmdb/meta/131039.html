Notes

Music compilation for the TV anime "Patalliro!"

Original Work: Mineo Maya
Serialization: Hana to Yume (HAKUSENSHA)

BGM Composer and Arranger: Nozomi Aoki (02~11, 13~18, 20~26, 28, 29, 31~33, 35, 36, 38)
BGM Composer: Mako Yuuki (30)
BGM Arranger: Nozomi Aoki (30)

Performer: Malynera Onion Orchestra (except tracks listed below)


01. Opening Theme
Vocals: Fusako Fujimoto
Lyricist and Composer: Kaoru Ito
Arranger: Nozomi Aoki

12. Insert Song
Vocals: Kazuyuki Sogabe (Bancoran / ハンコラン)
Lyricist and Composer: Yukinojo Mori
Arranger: Nozomi Aoki

19. Insert Song
Vocals: Toshiko Fujita (Maraich / マライヒ)
Lyricist: Kayoko Fuyumori
Composer and Arranger: Nozomi Aoki

27. Insert Song
Vocals: Slapstick
Dialogue: Fuyumi Shiraishi (Patalliro / パタリロ)
Lyricist: Yukinojo Mori
Composer: Koichi Morita
Arranger: Nozomi Aoki

34. 1st Ending Theme
Vocals: Eri Takeda
Lyricist and Composer: Kaoru Ito
Arranger: Nobuyuki Nakamura*

37. 2nd Ending Theme
Vocals: Slapstick, Fuyumi Shiraishi (Patalliro)
Lyricist: Nobu Tanaka
Composer: 宮庄順子, Nozomi Aoki
Arranger: Nozomi Aoki


*Credit known from other albums, Nozomi Aoki is wrongly credited for this track on the insert
