Notes

"You wake one morning to find a chill in the air. The sun isn't quite as bright as it should be. The plants and trees look weary, the clouds are darker, and strange things seem to be creeping just outside of your vision. A breeze rustles the dying leaves in the trees, and you suddenly realize... IT'S HALLOWEEN!

DANSE MACABRE 3 is the third annual horror tribute curated by Viking Guitar Productions. Featuring tons of musicians from the game remix scene, DM3 offers fresh takes on horror tunes, both familiar and obscure, old and new.

DM3 is free to download, but if any money is offered, we're donating it to a couple of special causes. All proceeds will go directly to Doctors Without Borders and Child's Play, two charities that the DM3 team feel very strongly about!

As always, the incredible cover art was created by FoxDragon. This year pays tribute to the classic 80's flight "Fright Night." We also have super-awesome liner artwork by Sindra as homage to "Tales From the Darkside.""

All tracks arranged, recorded and mixed by their respective musicians
Mastered by Erik Peabody (Viking Guitar)
