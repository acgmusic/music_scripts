Notes

This album was initially announced as a made-to-order product, available exclusively during two periods: from March 25 to May 7, 2024, and from May 30 to July 31, 2024. It later became generally available at select online retailers starting on October 17, 2024. The soundtrack is packaged in a tall digipack with a sleeve case and comes with a 40-page booklet featuring roundtable discussions with the sound team. Additionally, the album comes with a wooden photo frame music box that plays a 15-second arrangement of the main theme, "WELCOME TO THE NEW WORLD!", along with a set of 5 photo cards.
