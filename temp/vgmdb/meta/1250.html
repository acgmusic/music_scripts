Notes

The Discs in this box are Yellowish~Gold in color due to the Anniversary Box re-release, instead of the normal gray discs found in the original prints.

Box set reprint of five albums:
Disc 1: Cadash / Camel Try (PCCB-00043)
Disc 2: Space Gun / Mizubaku Adventure (PCCB-00052)
Disc 3: Runark / Gun Frontier (PCCB-00060)
Disc 4: Metal Black (PCCB-00079)
Disc 5: Pu.Li.Ru.La (PCCB-00091)

Composition: Pinch-Punch (disc 1), Norihiro Furukawa (disc 2), Naoto Yagishita (disc 2), Yasuhisa Watanabe (disc 3-5), Kazuko Umino (disc 5)
