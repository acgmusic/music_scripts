Notes

Packaged in special box case.

Notes from Parent Album:
Track 16, a bonus track for the album was released via a html link in the booklet of the album. This html link asked listeners to solve a riddle, however the link in the limited edition of the album did not give access to the track. Only the link provided in the regular edition of the album would provide access to a riddle which, upon solving, would lead to a download for the bonus track. The track details are listed below:

16 神の光 -Μοιρα- 7:06
English: Light of God -Moira-
Romaji: Kami no Hikari -Moira-


The track was included on "Chronology [2005-2010]" (KIZC-149~151).
