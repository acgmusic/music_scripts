Notes

Third CD of the Radio Program "WHITE ALBUM2 Doukoukai Radio" for PS3 Game "WHITE ALBUM2: Shiawase no Mukougawa" broadcasted on Internet Radio Station "Otoizumi".
  Disc 01 is an Audio CD with new recordings for the Program.
  Disc 02 is a MP3 data CD with recordings of the Radio Program from episodes 25 to 36th. It also includes recordings for "Dungeon Travelers 2 Ouritsu Tsuushin" to introduce
  "Dungeon Travelers 2: Ouritsu Toshokan to Mamono no Fuuin" as a box program.
