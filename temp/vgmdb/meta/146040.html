Notes

Singer-songwriter Midori Karashima's early best. A collection of early works including singles and songs from anime soundtracks released while she was with King Records, plus her debut songs released under the Victor label.

Album released in King Records "The Perfect Best Series" collection, and which looks like an extended version of the album "Memories" (KICS-113).
