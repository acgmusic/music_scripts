Notes

DONKEY ME OST
Reductions/Music by Gryzor87 (c) 2013

-Disclaimer-
Donkey Me is a non profit project oriented to pay homage to some popular 80's films.
There is no intention of improper use of third parties rights which may result from referring to games and/or films here recreated.
The tunes have been reduced to 3-mono-channels from their original recordings (orchestra/band) using a sinusoid waveform, creaing a similar DK original sound. Most of the pieces are excerps from original soundtracks, some of them are free adaptations and a few of them are original compositions.

TRIVIA: I sorted the tracks by order of making, and it resulted the 1st track of 'The Exorcist' the number 66 (!!) and the last one, the number 73 (year of this movie release!!)
