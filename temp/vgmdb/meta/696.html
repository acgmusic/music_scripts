Notes

OST and MIDI Arrange Versions of Contra Spirits (Contra III: The Alien Wars), with additional medleys from past games in the series.

01~13: Contra III: The Alien Wars (SNES)
- composition: Miki Yanagisawa, Masanori Adachi, Tappi Iwase, Aki Hata*
* Aki Hata is uncredited in the game, but says she composed two songs on her website.

14: Contra (Arcade)
- composition: Kazuki Muraoka

15: Super Contra (Arcade)
- composition: Kazuki Muraoka, Motoaki Furukawa

16: Contra (NES)
- composition: Hidenori Maezawa

17: Contra (MSX2)
- composition: unknown

18: Super C (NES)
- composition: Hidenori Maezawa

19: Operation C (GB)
- composition: Hidehiro Funauchi

20~24: Contra III: The Alien Wars (SNES) (MIDI Arrangement)
- composition: Miki Yanagisawa, Masanori Adachi, Tappi Iwase, Aki Hata
- arrangement: unknown
