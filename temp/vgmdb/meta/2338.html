Notes

The first 6 tracks are arranged.

Tracks 7-24 are from the original Famicom RPG by Namco, and tracks 25-51 are the SFX from the game.

The name of the game contains a pun where the first character of kaijuu (monster) is changed to the character of shell or shellfish, thus giving Shell Monsters.

The composer, Haruyoshi Rokudo is the Bass/Keyboards /Piano player from an 80's band called Anzen Chitai.
