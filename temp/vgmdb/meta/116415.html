Notes

"The Game Brass are proud to present 'Horns of Hyrule,' a celebration of the 35th anniversary of 'The Legend of Zelda' franchise. It's dangerous to go alone, so each track is arranged for a massive 16-piece brass ensemble. Featuring fan-favorite themes from each console game in the Zelda series, 'Horns of Hyrule' is sure to inspire the same spirit of fantasy, excitement, and adventure that has captivated audiences for generations."
