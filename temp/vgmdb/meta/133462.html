"Over 20 chiptune artists from around the world were brought together to create a unique, high-quality listening experience. The process began with one person composing a section, and others building upon it, resulting in an hour-long musical journey.

All 7 tracks were meticulously crafted using Dn-FamiTracker, a program that faithfully replicates the distinct sound characteristics of retro video game soundchips, particularly the NES/Famicom and its various Japan-exclusive expansion chips.

Watch the video to get a clear idea of how the album was distributed amongst the whole group"

---

"Big thank you to all who participated. This was mostly all you guys -- and your work, time, and passion are clearly shown here. ♡

We hope you enjoy! :)"
