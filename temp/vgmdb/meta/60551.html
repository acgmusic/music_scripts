Notes

Album containing the arranged background music played at the "Kirby Café" restaurants in Japan. At first it was exclusively sold at the restaurants, but was later sold commercially through ebten.

01. Game: Kirby's Dream Land
  Composition: Jun Ishikawa
  Arrangement: Hirokazu Ando

02. Game: Kirby's Adventure
  Composition & Arrangement: Hirokazu Ando

03. Game: Kirby Super Star
  Composition & Arrangement: Jun Ishikawa

04. Games: Kirby's Dream Land 2 (Cloudy Park), Kirby's Dream Land 3 (Ripple Field)
  Composition: Hirokazu Ando (Cloudy Park), Jun Ishikawa (Ripple Field)
  Arrangement: Megumi Ohara

05. Game: Kirby & The Amazing Mirror
  Composer unknown (music by Atsuyoshi Isemura & Hironobu Inagaki)
  Arrangement: Jun Ishikawa

06. Game: Kirby Air Ride
  Composition & Arrangement: Shogo Sakai

07. Game: Kirby's Return to Dream Land
  Composition & Arrangement: Hirokazu Ando

08. Game: Kirby Triple Deluxe
  Composition & Arrangement: Hirokazu Ando

09. Games: Dedede's Drum Dash Deluxe, Kirby: Canvas Curse, Kirby and the Rainbow Curse
  Composition: Hirokazu Ando (Dedede's Drum Dash Deluxe), Jun Ishikawa (Canvas Curse), unknown (Rainbow Curse; music composed by Shogo Sakai & Megumi Ohara)
  Arrangement: Megumi Ohara

10. Game: Kirby 64
  Composition & Arrangement: Jun Ishikawa

11. Game: Kirby Super Star
  Composition & Arrangement: Jun Ishikawa

12. Game: Kirby: Planet Robobot
  Composition & Arrangement: Hirokazu Ando
