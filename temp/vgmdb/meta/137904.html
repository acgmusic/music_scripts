Notes

01 Sonic the Hedgehog - Green Hill Zone (From 2004)

Composed by Masato Nakamura

This was part of a pitch for a project that didn’t happen. But when I listen to it now, I’m impressed that this is nearly 20
years old and it still jams. Love it!

02 Mega Man 8 - Search Me (feat. Anthony Lofton) (From 2004)

Featuring Anthony Lofton on Piano
Composed by Shusaku Uchiyama

So long ago that I don’t remember why I would dip into Mega Man 8, a game I have never played. But I really dig the vibe, and I love Anthony’s piano. I can always hear him, no matter what instrument he’s playing.

03 Animal Crossing - Soul Swatter (From 2008)

Composed by Toru Minegishi

This was my first attempt for the Joe_Cam “Animal Crossing” arrangement project. It didn’t quite hit right so I went back and made “I Got Soul” featuring Anthony Lofton on soprano sax. But I always thought this was pretty decent.
Reminded me of the 2nd Fly Swatter music in Mario Paint.

04 M&Ms Mini Madness - virt's Madness (From 2002)

Composed by Jake Kaufman

I made this years ago for OverClocked ReMix. I may not have been the first to do so, but I wanted to arrange something from a game that someone from the scene composed. Jake Kaufman (known as virt) went on to compose many excellent soundtracks for games, including fan favorite “Shovel Knight.”

05 M.C. Kids - po kidz (From 2001)

Composed by Charles Deenen

Posu Yan is one of my favorite arrangers to this day. I wanted to pay tribute to him. He often picked the games less traveled and I tried to emulate elements of his style. Plus, who knew the soundtrack to a McDonald’s game would be so incredible?

06 The Legend of Zelda - Lean Green Link Machine (From 2012)

Composed by Koji Kondo

This is the demo that I made for The OneUps so that they would understand the groove I wanted for what ultimately became “I Do It For the Faeries” from the “Intergalactic Continuum” album.

07 Final Fantasy VI - I Got That Magitek, Girl (From 2011)

Composed by Nobuo Uematsu

I really liked the idea of a sexxy Magitek factory music rendition, and while I like what I started here, I never found the right esper to go with it. Still a great groove.

08 Chrono Trigger - The Dreamer Awakens (From 2002)

Composed by Yasunori Mitsuda

2002 was a prolific and important year for me. It seems like I was churning out music at a ferocious pace. Chrono Trigger remains a huge inspiration. Never found the right idea for this, but this is a fun listen in its own right.

09 Chrono Trigger - Palace (From 2009)

Composed by Yasunori Mitsuda

This is an abandoned demo from the Bad Dudes’ CHRONOTORIOUS album. Ultimately, it was too simple and laid back for such a notoriously hard-hitting album. It’s a cool vibe, though.

10 Chrono Trigger - Secrets (From 2009)

Composed by Yasunori Mitsuda

This is the precursor to the “Forest Steppin’” track on the Bad Dudes’ CHRONOTORIOUS album. You can hear how far I got with this version, but it was just too harsh. I’m so glad that I went back and restarted by keeping some ideas, and taking even more direct influence from MJ Cole. This is a fun listen, but my final track is one I listen to over and over to this day.

11 Chrono Trigger - Schala for Steph (From 2016)

Composed by Yasunori Mitsuda

A track that didn’t make the cut from my album, THEW ORLD ISSQ UARE. I was really working hard on thisl I had every intention of having someone replace the flute, but I couldn’t figure out where to go. However, the major chords inspired the next abandoned demo, “Schala.”

12 Chrono Trigger - Schala (From 2017)

Composed by Yasunori Mitsuda

A track that didn’t make the cut from my album THEW ORLD ISSQ UARE. I could never figure out how to continue “Schala for Steph” but the major chords in that arrangement gave me this great idea to flip the track into a major key. I bet a lot of people would find this blasphemous, but if it wasn’t wildly different, it wouldn’t be a Mustin track. Still, I never found the full spark to finish this one.

13 The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass - Linebeck (From 2008)

Composed by Kenta Nagata

I think Linebeck might be my favorite Zelda character. I love how his pompous theme fits his personality. I wanted to make a version of his music that wasn’t just swashbuckling, but the rest of the groove never hit me. But it’s still fun to listen to this little taste of a rizzed up Linebeck.

14 Final Fantasy VII - Lifestream (From 2017)

Composed by Nobuo Uematsu

A track that didn’t make the cut from my album THEW ORLD ISSQ UARE. When I asked Jayson Napolitano of Scarlet Moon Records to be the Executive Producer on my album, I asked if he had any requests. He chose “Lifestream,” but I couldn’t find a way to make it fit into the groove of the album. The mood is pretty cool, though. Maybe it’ll inspire you.

15 Castlevania - Heart of Fire Vamp (From 2001)

Composed by Kinuyo Yamashita

In the early days of the video game music arrangement scene, I was trying all kinds of stuff. “Heart of Fire” has always been a favorite Castlevania tune of mine. While this version is very cool and different, I never found a way to continue on and finish it. Thankfully, I did finally do the track in 2021 as “Hearth of Fire” for the Scarlet Moon Christmas release that year. Whereas this track is funky with a weird meter, the version I ultimately made sounds like a Hallmark
Christmas Movie soundtrack. Do check it out.

16 Super Mario Series - Super Mario R.A.P. (From 2004)

Composed by Koji Kondo

I wanted to hear a rap with Luigi, Toad, Mario, and Bowser, so I put together a demo of what their sections would sound like. Never did get that rap to happen, but this is pretty cool. That Bowser beat tho!

17 Super Mario Series - I, Mario Project Bid (From 2005)

Composed by Koji Kondo

In 2005, Shael Riley asked, “What would the Mario series be like if it was a dystopian horror game?” It was eventually dubbed, “I, Mario,” and the message boards came alive with all manner of amazing short stories and incredible artwork. I created this demo of what the music could potentially sound like. Now, in 2023, we have seen many examples of what this might be, but in 2005, we were having a great time using our imaginations.

18 Final Fantasy IV - Napolitano Beauty Ringtone (From 2017)

Composed Nobuo Uematsu

For Jayson Napolitano’s birthday in 2017, I made him this ringtone as a gift. His favorite Final Fantasy IV music is “Troian Beauty” so I cooked this up for him. Feel free to use it yourself.

19 Sunset Riders Fangamer Commercial - 01 Greedwell's (From 2018)

Composed by Mustin

I was asked by video game merch company Fangamer to create the soundtrack for an officially sanctioned commercial for the Konami game “Sunset Riders.” I played this game so much throughout my life so I had a blast. This tune is an original composition that sets the mood.

20 Sunset Riders Fangamer Commercial - 02 Simon (From 2018)

Original Title: “Omae mo kore made da !”
Composed by Motoaki Furukawa

It was so nice to hear this tune with some real instruments. I got to make it just as I heard it in my head all these years.
William Carlos Reyes on guitars. David Ortiz on trumpets.

21 Sunset Riders Fangamer Commercial - 03 Sunset (From 2018)

Original Title: “Men of the wilderness”
Composed by Motoaki Furukawa

Being able to reconstruct this piece into an Ennio Morricone Spaghetti Western style arrangement was a dream come true. I loved the idea of reversing the sections, which worked out perfect for the video. I REALLY wanted to go back and make this a full arrangement but my project file IS CORRUPTED! Not very often that this happens but them's the breaks. That said, the second section is one of my most proud musical accomplishments of my life so far. Hopefully I’ll
be able to revisit this style again. David Ortiz on trumpets. William Carlos Reyes on guitar in the second section.

22 The Legend of Zelda 2002 - Link's Theme

From “The Legend of Zelda: Link’s Awakening”
Composed by Koji Kondo & Kozue Ishikawa

An isolated audio section from the audio drama “The Legend of Zelda 2002” from New Year’s Day 2002. I wanted to make an energetic version of the Zelda theme since it would be the only time we hear it in the audio drama. It also includes the additional section from the “Link’s Awakening” version that I love so much. During this part of the audio drama, Link is riding Epona to save princess Zelda.

23 The Legend of Zelda 2002 - In the Dungeon

From “The Legend of Zelda”
Composed by Koji Kondo

An isolated audio section from the audio drama “The Legend of Zelda 2002” from New Year’s Day 2002. One of my top favorite pieces of video game music. An extended version of this eventually made its way to the “More Than Mario” Album from 2006.

24 The Legend of Zelda 2002 - Run

From “The Legend of Zelda: Link’s Awakening”
Composed by Kazumi Totaka

An isolated audio section from the audio drama “The Legend of Zelda 2002” from New Year’s Day 2002. Another favorite from “Link’s Awakening.” Gotta go fast!

25 The Legend of Zelda 2002 - Ganon's Castle

From “The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past”
Composed by Koji Kondo

An isolated audio section from the audio drama “The Legend of Zelda 2002” from New Year’s Day 2002. It was fun to make such a different take on the theme. To go from brooding to break beats - so much fun.

26 The Legend of Zelda 2002 - Battle with Ganon

From “The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past”
Composed by Koji Kondo

An isolated audio section from the audio drama “The Legend of Zelda 2002” from New Year’s Day 2002. This is one of my favorite arrangements that I’ve made. It’s just so driving and exciting. I think that piano bass line is one of Kondo’s best. I find it faithful but with a modern twist.

27 The Legend of Zelda 2002 - Boss Clear

From “The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past”
Composed by Koji Kondo

An isolated audio section from the audio drama “The Legend of Zelda 2002” from New Year’s Day 2002. A good Zelda ringtone if you need one.

28 The Legend of Zelda 2002 - Princess Rescued
From “The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past”
Composed by Koji Kondo

An isolated audio section from the audio drama “The Legend of Zelda 2002” from New Year’s Day 2002. A good Zelda ringtone if you need one. Maybe even a text notification sound.
