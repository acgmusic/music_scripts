Notes

"BATTLE CLiMAX" is a medley of various BGM played as the final battle of the poke series against the champion, against those who have surpassed the champion, and as the first offensive and defensive battle in which the champion hero is challenged. Now comes its latest edition, 2022!
In addition to the addition of new medleys from BW2 to Sword and Shield, long versions of each song are also included!
In addition, "BATTLE CLiMAX" distributed in 2011 is included as a bonus disc.

All arranged by hapi⇒
