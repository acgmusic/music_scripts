Notes

From The World Revolving to the mighty God Shattering Star, all 2020 GaMetal songs are here!
This release also includes 5 bonus tracks: an instrumental version of Life Will Change (Persona 5), and 2021 remasters of the old 2015 and 2017 GaMetal remixes Dark Meta Knight (Kirby & The Amazing Mirror), Area 6 (Star Fox 64), Natural Killer Cyborg / Unfounded Revenge (Mother 3), and One-Winged Angel (Final Fantasy VII)!
