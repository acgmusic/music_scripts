Notes

01: Dragon Quest V [Enix]
Composer: Koichi Sugiyama

02~04: Axelay [Konami]
Composer: Kukeiha Club (Taro Kudo)

05~09: Lennus (Paladin's Quest) [Asmik]
Composer: Kohei Tanaka

10~11: Sonic Blast Man [Taito]
Composer: ZUNTATA (Katsuhisa Ishikawa)

12~14: Garou Densetsu (Fatal Fury) [Takara]
These songs are from the original Neo-Geo version of the game, not the SFC port.
Composer: Shinsekai Gakkyoku Zatsugidan
    KONNY: 12, 13
    Toshikazu Tanaka: 14

15: Kinnikuman [Yutaka]
Composer: TOGASHI NORIHIKO

16~18: Lord Monarch [Epoch]
Track 16 is an arrangement of "Menu" from the original PC-98 version of Lord Monarch. Tracks 17 & 18 are SNES original compositions.
Composer: Falcom Sound Team J.D.K., E.S.C.

19: Shinchou Kouki [Yanoman]
Composer: Masamichi Iwasaki

20~22: Return of Double Dragon (Super Double Dragon) [Technos Japan]
Composer: Kazunaka Yamane

23~27: Aguri Suzuki F1 Super Driving (Redline F-1 Racer) [Logic]
Composer: Katsuhiro Hayashi

28: Great Strategy Expert [ASCII]
Composer: Akiko Hashimoto

29~31: SD Kidou Senshi Gundam [Angel]
Composer: Studio Kumataro (Yuki Sabakuma)
Track 29 arranger unknown.

32~35: SUPER PANG (Super Buster Bros.) [Capcom]
Track 32 is erroneously marked as being from the arcade version of the game, when it's really a SNES original composition. Track 33 is based on a theme from the arcade version, while the other tracks are original compositions.
Arcade Composer: Alfh Lyra (Hiromitsu Takaoka)
SNES composer & arranger unknown; sound designed by Tatsuya Nishimura & Minae Fujii

36: Song Master [Yanoman]
Composer: Tsuyoshi Tanaka, Koji Abe

37: Teitoku no Ketsudan (P.T.O.: Pacific Theater of Operations) [KOEI]
Composer: Hiroshi Miyagawa

38: Super Daikoukai Jidai (Uncharted Waters) [KOEI]
Composer: Yoko Kanno

39: Assault Suits Valken (Cybernator) [NCS]
Composer: Opus Studio Inc. (Masanao Akahori)

40~45: Super Mario Kart [Nintendo]
Composer: Soyo Oka
