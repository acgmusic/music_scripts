Notes

The legendary ActRaiser soundtrack composed by Yûzô Koshiro! The 'Master' is back for the 30th anniversary with this divine 2 x CD Edition! Features bonus unused tracks and the new Symphonic Suite concert that was recorded during Ancient Festival in 2018!

The CD Edition features

- 2 CD (Picture Disc) with the original soundtrack and the Symphonic Suite.
- A new 8 pages booklet with comments from composer Yûzô Koshiro and the Symphonic Suite team (Yûsuke Ichihara, Nijûhachi Haneda)
- New original illustrations by Ayano Koshiro (who worked on several games design with her brother, including ActRaiser!)
