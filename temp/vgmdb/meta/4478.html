Notes

While the content of this album is mostly a reprint of 32DH-5285; this version featured digital remastering, includes a longer version of "THE WORLD OF MOTHER" with more of the game's original music, and an additional track (track 12), which is an arranged version of "SMILES AND TEARS" from Mother 2.
