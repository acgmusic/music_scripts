Track 31 is a hidden track starting after roughly 8 minutes of silence on track 30.

Although Mark Miller is officially credited for composing all music for Earthworm Jim, Tommy Tallarico later stated in an interview he was the one who composed it, and that him and Miller had agreed to use Miller's name for credits so as to avoid any legal issues with Virgin.
