Notes

To commemorate the 35th anniversary of the release of the first episode, a limited production "Bubblegum Crisis 35th BOX" is released, which includes the complete "Bubblegum Crisis DVD Collection BOX" released in 2002 on two Blu-ray Discs. The soundtracks of all eight episodes are also included on a high-quality CD (MQA-CD), remastered from the original master tapes.
