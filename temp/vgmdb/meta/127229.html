Notes

This is an addition to the album "Unknown Pseudoalbum", but it's not exactly a second disc of the same album but more like a separated extension of it, with different title as well, so I guess it makes more sense to add it separately too.

All tracks arranged, produced, mixed, ruined and whatever by Unknown Pseudoartist at Chocobo Studios between 2021 - 2023.

Development of Avoid - lots of weird vocals at track 3
Ian Martyn - Arrangement base at track 27
Ivan Hakstok - croatian warning at track 1
Jinx - Piano at track 28
Katajun - English warning at track 1
Max Noel - French warning at track 1
Newmajoe - Lead keys at track 18
TheManPF - Spanish warning at track 1
Zeonicfreak - Kazoos and meows at track 12 & 14, improvised lyrics and vocals at track 12, DJ acting at track 4, acapella guitars at track 19
