Notes

Track 54 borrows the instrumental part of track 56, composed by Seiko Kobuchi.

56 Composition: Seiko Kobuchi / Vocals: Capricious Comet (Capcom) / Lyrics: Makoto Ikehara [池原 まこと]

57 Composition & Arrangement: Yoshimasa Inoue / Vocals: Asami Abe / Lyrics: Asami Abe, Natsumi Watanabe


* The following information is taken directly from the booklet and is potentially incorrect. The booklet incorrectly lists Capricious Comet as having sung track 55 instead of 56, and it fails to properly credit Seiko Kobuchi for composing tracks 54 and 56. The numbering may at some point be off by one.
