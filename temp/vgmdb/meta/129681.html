Notes

Complete original soundtrack on 2 CD to the movie "Sengoku Jieitai" (1979). Kentaro Haneda's score, long thought to have been lost, has finally been discovered more than 40 years after its release.
In addition to the music and songs used in the movie on Disc 1, Disc 2 contains the unused score.
All previously released songs are included and have been remastered.

Music: Kentaro Haneda
