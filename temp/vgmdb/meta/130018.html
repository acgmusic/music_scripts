Notes

A two-disc compilation album that brings together the most electrifying and awe-inspiring video game music remixes of 2021 from the online game music remix competition, Dwelling of Duels.

All of these tracks were submitted to a monthly competition during 2021, and are available for free on our website. This compilation brings together what we think is a wonderful cross-section of our community, and any purchases support the artists.

Disc 1 (Tracks 1-13)
Disc 2 (Tracks 14-25)

Includes 12 bonus tracks not available on other music platforms!
