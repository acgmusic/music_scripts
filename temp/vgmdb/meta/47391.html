Notes

On the physical release's first disc, the music data of tracks 9 and 10 were mistakenly swapped with that of tracks 19 and 20. This is confirmed by the game's option to change the background music in Ion's room. The correct track titles can be seen in the digital version of this album.

A bonus track (titled 'Ciel Nosurge', length 0:56) was given to those who pre-ordered the digital version of the album, and to those who answered the album's questionnaire in Gust's social website. Arranged/composer for this bonus track is Akiko Shikata (uncredited in the booklet).

Disc 2 is entirely composed of unused tracks for the game.
