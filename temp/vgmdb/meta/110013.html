Notes

This 2-CD set comes in an LP-size box, and each CD comes in its own jewel case. Disc 1 is the Seiyou Shinma-hen Drama Album 6 [PCCG-435]; Disc 2 is the Seiyou Shinma-hen Ongaku-hen 2 [PCCG-00437]. Audio content for the respective CDs is the same. There are two empty slots in the box, presumably for the 4th and 5th drama albums.
