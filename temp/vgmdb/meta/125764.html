Notes

A 2-disc album containing a total of 33 songs from the anime "Marmalade Boy" aired in 1994-95. Includes the theme song "Egao ni Aitai" and character songs by Mariko Kouda, Sakura Tange, Kikuko Inoue, Ryotaro Okiayu, Aya Hisakawa and others.

Compared to previous editions, the song "RAIN" is provided in its complete length.
