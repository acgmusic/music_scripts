Notes

Tracks 7 and 27 are misspelled as "VIG VIPER" and "DINOSAIR" on one part of the packaging but spelled correctly elsewhere.

01~05: Captain Tsubasa III [Tecmo]
Composer unknown; music composed by Ryuichi Nitta, Machine Rio, Rika Shigeno, & Kaori Nakabai

06~09: Parodius Da! [Konami]
Each of these four tracks is a short jingle followed by an original main melody written for the arcade version of the game. Tracks 6 & 8 share one main melody, and tracks 7 & 9 share another.
06 jingle: Originally from TwinBee, composer unknown
07 jingle: Originally from Gradius, composed by Miki Higashino
08 jingle: Original composition from the arcade version, composed by Kazuki Muraoka
09 jingle: Originally from Antarctic Adventure, composer unknown
Arcade composer: Kazuki Muraoka
SNES arranger unknown; sound designed by Kazuhiko Uehara & Hideyuki Eto

10~13: Light Fantasy [Tonkinhouse]
Composer unknown; sound by Michiharu Hasuya, Osamu Kasai (笠井 治), & Takashi Tsurutani

14~15: Kouryuu Densetsu Villgust [Bandai]
Composed: Shinichi Tanaka

16: Prince of Persia [NCS]
Composer unknown; music by Toshiya Yamanaka & Tetsuya Nakano

17~21: SD Gundam X [Yutaka]
Composer unknown; sound by Tomoyuki Hamada & Masas.

22~23: Hiryuu no Ken S [Culture Brain]
Music: Akinori Sawa

24~27: T.M.N.T. (Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles IV: Turtles in Time) [Konami]
Arcade music composer: Mutsuhiko Izumi
SNES arranger unknown; sound designed by Kazuhiko Uehara & Harumi Ueko

28~30: ASTRAL BOUT [King Records]
Composition unknown; sound by Takuo Uchida, Yukiyoshi Fujimoto, & Jun Otsuki

31~33: The Addams Family [Misawa Entertainment]
Music: Jonathan Dunn
Tracks 31 & 32 incorporate the theme song from The Addams Family, originally composed by Vic Mizzy

34~36: Cameltry (On the Ball) [Taito]
Original arcade composer: Pinch-Punch
SNES sound: ZUNTATA

37~38: Koushien 2 [K. Amusement Leasing]
Composer unknown

39~41: Super F1 Circus [Nihon Bussan]
Music Composer: Hiroshi Ogawa

42: Ultimate Football (Football Fury) [Sammy]
Sound Composer: Megumi Matsuura

43: PGA Tour Golf [Imagineer]
Original Genesis audio & SNES music: Rob Hubbard

44: Super Dunk Shot (NCAA Basketball) [HAL Laboratory]
Composer unknown; music & sounds by H. Kingsley Thurber, Paul Webb, & Mark Ganus

45~46: Yuuyu no Quiz de GOGO! [Taito]
Original arcade music: Tamayo Kawamoto
SNES arranger unknown; sound by Tamayo Kawamoto, Kiyohiro Sada, & Masako Inata (稲田 昌子)

47: Song Master [Yanoman]
Composer unknown; music composed by Tsuyoshi Tanaka & Koji Abe

48: F1 GRAND PRIX [Video System]
Music Composer: Naoki Itamura

49~53: Street Fighter II [Capcom]
Composer: Yoko Shimomura
SNES arranger unknown; sound designed by Yoshihiro Sakaguchi, Yoko Shimomura, Tatsuya Nishimura, & Isao Abe

54~57: Maka Maka [Sigma]
Sound Artist: Mitsuyasu Tomohisa
These tracks are arranged by an uncredited arranger.
