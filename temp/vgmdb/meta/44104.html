This album contains music from the Asgard arc of the TV series (1~4), from the second movie "Kamigami no Atsuki Tatakai" (5,11), from the Poseidon arc of the TV series (6~8), from the first movie "Jashin Eris" (9), from the third movie "Shinku no Shounen Densetsu" (12), and from the fourth movie "Saishuu Seisen no Senshitachi" (13,14). Track 5 has been reprised also in the second TV series, the beginning of track 10 contains the Shun's theme reprised on the TV series, the main Orpheus Lyra's theme from first movie and a little part of Mime's melody from the Asgard arc of the TV series.
