Notes

The album does not provide a composer/game breakdown. Several members of the games' sound teams give comments in the booklet and their works are listed, but the games may also have composers who weren't interviewed.


DISC 1


01~13: Golden Axe (1989, Arcade / SYSTEM 16B)
Commentator: Tohru Nakabayashi
Yousuke Okunari only says that Y.T wrote music in the liner notes of SEGA SYSTEM 16 COMPLETE SOUNDTRACK Vol.3 [WM-0681~3]. Nakabayashi only comments on programming and sound effects in the booklet of this album.

Credits from the digital release:
Composer:
  SEGA (01~13)
Arranger:
  SEGA (01~13)

14~33: Golden Axe The Revenge of Death Adder (1992, Arcade / SYSTEM 32)
Commentators: Masanori Takeuchi, Tomoyuki Kawamura
Game has no credits.
Kawamura comments he worked on 16, 17, 18 and 23 before being transferred to work on HOLOSSEUM.

Credits from digital release:

  Tomoyuki Kawamura (17, 18, 23)

01~13: Golden Axe (1989, Sega Mega Drive / Sega Genesis)
No commentators.

14~29: Golden Axe II (1991, Sega Mega Drive / Sega Genesis)
Commentator: Naofumi Hataya
Game credits "sound effect" to Hatabaw.
Hataya's old Wave Master profile says he did all music and sound effects.

Credits from digital release:
Composer:
  Naofumi Hataya (14~16, 19~30)
  Masafumi Ogata (17, 18)
Arranger:
  Naofumi Hataya (14~16, 19~30)
  Masafumi Ogata (17, 18)


30~55: Golden Axe III (1993, Sega Mega Drive / Sega Genesis)
Commentators: Naofumi Hataya, Tatsuyuki Maeda
Game credits "music and sound effect" to N.H (Naofumi Hataya), Ryunosuke (Tatsuyuki Maeda), Lotty (Haruyo Oguro), and T.S. (Tomonori Sawada)
Hataya's old Wave Master profile says he did music. Maeda's says he did music and sound effects. Hataya confirmed in May 2015 that "Wilderness" was composed by Tatsuyuki Maeda and "Ride The Whirlwind" was composed by himself. Sawada confirmed in January 2016 that "Boss Odd Stage" is the only track he composed, however the digital release additionally credits him for "The Scorching Sand".

Credits from digital release:
