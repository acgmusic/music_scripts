Notes

Original soundtrack from the TV series "Himitsu Sentai Gorenger", the first Super Sentai series.

The BGM was first commercialized in the album "Himitsu Sentai Gorenger MUSIC COLLECTION" (COCC-13265) in 1996, but this 2-disc set includes previously unreleased tracks and 2016 new digital remastering.
The first press includes a B5 size pencil board.

Tracks released for the first time:
DISC 1 Tr.21~28,31~34,36~38,41~43,49~51
DISC 2 Tr.5~8,13,20~24,32~47,60,65~71
