Note:
  The Credits breakdown was made based on other album releases.

  M-1.28 and 1.29 are not initially viewable during normal playback or selectable on the chapter selection screen. In order to view them, while on the main menu screen, press up, up, down, down, right, right, left, and left with the relevant device (remote control, game controller, etc...). This code is available on a screen after the credits.
Once the command has been input correctly, the beginning of M-1.28 will automatically play. M-1.28 and 1.29 basically "append" themselves to the end of the normal run-time, thus extending the total run-time to a little over two hours. M-1.28 and 1.29 will be continually available for normal playback until the DVD is ejected.
