Containing the six previous Hymmnos vocal albums with a DVD with 5.1ch mix music, the OVA add-on CD, a preview of the soundtrack for Ciel nosurge, three new songs written by Akiko Shikata, Haruka Shimotsuki, Noriko Mitose, and a setting materials book. Each one of the three new songs will have a character the singers voiced in the series appointed to sing it, which will be chosen through a poll in the Ar Portal site.
