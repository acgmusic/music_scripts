Notes

Virtual Video Game Orchestra and Game Music Ensemble at UCLA are proud to present: "ADVENTURE AWAITS! A Sea of Thieves Medley" and credits arrangement "BECALMED". These arrangements are our biggest yet, with over 100 distinct parts performed on over 300 recordings by 75 musicians from across the global video game music community.

"ADVENTURE AWAITS! A Sea of Thieves Medley"
from Sea of Thieves (2018)
Composed by Robin Beanland
Arranged by John Hovig Tabakian

"BECALMED"
from Sea of Thieves (2018)
Composed by Robin Beanland
Arranged by John Hovig Tabakian
