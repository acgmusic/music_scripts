On the album cover, Nagisa is holding a shikishi drawn by Itaru Hinoue. Though this album features the first 13 weeks of the radio show, it wouldn't be until episode 21 that the shikishi would be unveiled and given to a lucky listener. Its depiction garnered attention, as Nagisa is wearing Sanae's sweater and apron, while Sanae is wearing Nagisa's winter uniform.
