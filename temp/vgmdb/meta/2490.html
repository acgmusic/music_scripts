Notes

The second disc is a first print bonus.

Tommy Tallarico, Todd Dennis, Shane Kneip and Chris Rickwood arranged and orchestrated the Level 1 theme of Ghosts n Goblins in various ways on Disc1. Tamayo Kawamoto did the FMV music on Disc 1. Ayako Mori (Makaimura aka Ghosts n Goblins (1985)) and Tamayo Kawamoto (Daimakaimura aka Ghouls n Ghosts (1988)) composed the music on Disc 2.

Track 124 "Voice Collection" consists of "Prince Maximo, God of Death Grim, Evil King Achille, Lenore, Mamba Marie, Aurora Lee, Sophie, Dark Sophia".

Disc 2: Daimakaimura Bonus Disc (Ghouls n Ghosts - 1988)
