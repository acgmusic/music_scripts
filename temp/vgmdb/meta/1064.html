Notes

Arranged PlayStation2 Soundtrack
------------------------------------------------------------
One of the most sought after TEKKEN soundtracks in it's native country of release.
------------------------------------------------------------
The last TEKKEN arranged PlayStation soundtrack to feature compositions from the final 2 remaining members of the original "TEKKEN Sound Team". (Nobuyoshi Sano ~AND~ Keiichi Okabe)
------------------------------------------------------------
Tracks: 1, 4, 6, 10, 14, 26, 27, 28, 29, 33 Composed & Arranged by Akitaka Tohyama
Tracks: 2, 7, 11, 15, 21, 30 Composed & Arranged by Keiichi Okabe
Tracks: 3, 8, 12, 16, 31 Composed & Arranged by Yuu Miyake
Tracks: 5, 9, 13, 17, 32 Composed & Arranged by Nobuyoshi Sano
Tracks: 18 Composed by Yuu Miyake & Arranged by Keiichi Okabe
Tracks: 22 Composed by Akitaka Tohyama & Arranged by Satoru Kosaki
Tracks: 23 Composed & Arranged by Satoru Kosaki
Tracks: 24, 25 Composed by Rio Hamamoto & Arranged by Satoru Kosaki
Tracks: 19, 20 Composed & Arranged by Yoshihito Yano
