"Fragments of Gratitude is KodamaSounds' tribute album dedicated to the When They Cry series, one of the most beloved visual novel series of all time, and other 07th Expansion games. This immense universe is loved by all kinds of people, of all kinds of horizons from all around the world. This album is meant as an homage album to celebrate it all in music. From arrangements to original image songs, wether you're a fan of the series or not, you can be sure that you will appreciate those tunes!"
