Notes

VGM CON presents the biggest charity album jam it’s ever had! Vol. 4 consists of 18 cover songs spanning a wide variety of games and genres. All tracks were arranged, recorded, and mixed in 72 hours. Come visit us at the Sunshine Resort and celebrate the official album release!

This album benefits the PFund Foundation.

Produced by Ian Martyn, V-Ron Media
Album art by TheToader
Album mastering by James C. Hoffman
Special thanks to JamesTheLudomusicologist, Lucas Guimaraes, Josh Pendlebury, Rusty Powers
