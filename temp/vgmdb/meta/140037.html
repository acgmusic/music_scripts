Notes

Rosemary Butler's first solo album
This album was first released in Japan with a different cover and a different tracklist compared to the american market. The tracks "RIDING HIGH", "THE LAST HERO", "WHEN A MAN LOVES A WOMAN" and "CHILDREN OF THE LIGHT" are exclusive to the japanese print and have been replaced by 4 other songs for the american release (catalog: ST-12302).
