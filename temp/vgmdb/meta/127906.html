Notes

"This Original Soundtrack contains 9 CDs of 142 tracks that were used in Xenoblade Chronicles 3, released by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch on July 29, 2022. On top of the music included in the main game, music from Xenoblade Chronicles 3: Future Redeemed is also included. The package features an illustration made specifically for the soundtrack by character designer Masatsugu Saito. Continuing on from Xenoblade Chronicles and Xenoblade Chronicles 2, Yasunori Mitsuda, ACE (TOMOri Kudo, CHiCO), Kenji Hiramatsu and Manami Kiyota gathered again to work on the music for this soundtrack."

- from the soundtrack's official English-language website
