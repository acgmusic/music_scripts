Notes

Compilation album featuring exclusive music from a number of metal, industrial and electronica bands inspired by the first Mortal Kombat movie.
Alien Factory's "Higher" later turned up in the second movie, Mortal Kombat: Annihilation, Psykosonik's "It Has Begun" and Sister Machine Gun's
"Deeper Down" appeared in the animated series Mortal Kombat: Defenders of the Realm
