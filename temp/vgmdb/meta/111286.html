Notes

Virtual Video Game Orchestra is proud to present our ninth project, "The Republic of Bastok" from Final Fantasy XI, featuring 188 community submissions from musicians across the global video game music community.

— JOIN OUR COMMUNITY —
Discord server: discord.gg/vvgo

— LINKS —
Website: vvgo.org
Bandcamp: vvgo.bandcamp.com
Facebook: fb.me/virtualvgo
Instagram: instagram.com/virtualvgo
Twitter: twitter.com/virtualvgo
Twitch: twitch.tv/virtualvgo
Github: github.com/virtual-vgo/vvgo

The purpose of VVGO is to provide a fun and accessible virtual community of musicians from around the world by performing video game music.
All instruments, musical backgrounds, and skill levels are welcome to join!
