Notes

Disc 2 is the soundtrack for a game that wouldn't be released for some months!

On the disc 1, track 9 and track 11 titles are erroneously swapped. tr9 is actually "Op.13" and tr11 is actually "Slash". This has been confirmed with the game's sound test. The correct titles can also be seen on KMUS-0001, LC-1609~10, LC-1715 / GFCA-153, and GFCA-195~213.
