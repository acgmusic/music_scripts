Notes

The Cowboy Bebop Ultimate Edition was released in celebration of the 20th anniversary of Cowboy Bebop.

The Ultimate Edition contains the entire television series on Blu-ray, Cowboy Bebop The Movie, the Cowboy Bebop television series soundtrack, and a 120-page artbook, all packaged in a limited edition, individually numbered, vinyl flight case.

The set is limited to 1000 copies, and is exclusive to AllTheAnime.com and Zavvi.com.

The soundtrack contains 3 CD discs, "Cowboy Bebop" (Soundtrack 1), "Cowboy Bebop NO DISC", and "Cowboy Bebop BLUE".

The set was delayed to 31 December 2018 due to production issues.
