Notes

A compilation of soundtrack material from the 1983-84 tokusatsu series Space Sheriff Sharivan.

● Composer / Chumei Watanabe
● Arranger / Chumei Watanabe (DISC1, DISC2-9~12), Nobuyoshi Koshibe/Takashi Kokubo (DISC2-1~8)
● Lyricist / Keisuke Yamakawa (DISC1-1, 9, 14, 16, 21, 26, DISC2-12)
● Vocals / Akira Kushida (DISC1-1, 9, 14, 16, 21, 26, DISC2-12)

The booklet contains the following text:
- An interview with composer Chumei Watanabe (conducted at Mr. Watanabe's house on Thursday, Aug 31, 2006)
- Archived song commentary by Mr. Watanabe [from the liner notes of '[CQ-7081] Space Sheriff Sharivan Music Collection' (released 1983.7.1)]
- An archived interview with Mr. Watanabe about TV-sized music [from the liner notes of '[COCX-31984] TV Size! Metal Hero Complete Theme Song Collection' (released 2002.10.19)]
- An interview with music selector Koji Murata
