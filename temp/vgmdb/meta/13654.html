The first two discs feature segments from the Square Enix radio show "Uematsu Radio", and the third disc is a bonus cd featuring selected fan-arrangements of tracks from the Final Fantasy series. The cds came in a thin foldout cardboard case with no separate liner notes or obi. It was only obtainable via Uematsu's personal fan-club.
