Notes

Two disc set divided in the following way:

Disc 1 will be a vocal CD, including all of the songs that came in the special CDs bundled with the special editions of the manga tomes for Tindharia no Tane and Griotte no Nemuri Hime, as well as the two Tindharia no Tane Drama CDs and the special CD that came bundled with the special edition of the novelization for Griotte no Nemuri Hime. It will also include karaoke versions for each of these songs.

Disc 2 will be an instrumental CD containing all of the BGMs used in all of these five drama CDs, for a total of 22 tracks.

Will be released during Haruka Shimotsuki's FEL FEARY WEL Original Fantasy Concert 2012, and sold on the CRAFTSCAPE shop this July 7th.

All vocals & chorus performed by Haruka Shimotsuki.
